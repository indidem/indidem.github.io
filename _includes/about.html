Shirley Bongers' Family History Research


Shirley has been researching her extended family history for over 30 years.

In that time she's identified around 12,000 individuals, from over 2,100 families, and across 6 centuries.


This website forms part of the Indidem* project, which aims to make Shirley's research accessible, plus enable it to remain alive, current and ongoing.

The other aspects of the project are detailed in the Achievements section, below.

* Indidem is a Latin word meaning from the same place or source.

The research collection

Data

Shirley recorded her research in several databases; so far the most current database has been analysed, which has 11,451 entries and mentions 12,184 people.

Records

Shirley's substantial collection of research notes and documentary evidence are due for analysis, with a view to digitising and archiving.

Artefacts

Shirley's collection also includes a range of artefacts includes historical photos, personal items and other inherited keepsakes.
