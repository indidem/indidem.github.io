Narrative

TROVE - Courier Mail, Brisbane Wed. 21 April, 1954, Page 8. "HAS BEEN A CLERIC FOR 66 YEARS"
Time has made little difference to the Rev. John Bongers, Queensland's oldest Congregational Minister. He will be 93yrs next month, 66 years a minister -- and still comes willingly out of retirement at Wynnum to take a church service.
Mr. Bongers was ordained in 1888, he has served in N.S.W., Victoria, & Tasmania before coming to Queensland. He preached in Queensland for 43 years, before he retired in 1938.

Notice in Courier Mail, Brisbane - Rev John BONGERS, at Brisbane Hospital, late of 23 Clare St., Wynnum, Brisbane. Cremated 3 June, 1957 at Mt Thompson, Brisbane, wife of Eva Amy Bongers, children John Frazer, Mrs.A.Cunliffe, Edward Malcolm & Ralph Lincoln & Step-children (of Eva Amy). - 1957/BO 19021.
