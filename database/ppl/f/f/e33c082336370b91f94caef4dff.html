Narrative

When I was a small child, I was about 5yrs old, I can remember our whole family, my mother,father & my little brother, visiting Aunt Ada at Cronulla, I can't remember why we went. My mother said she used to like Aunt Ada, she & her daughters did beautiful crochet work. When I was in England in 1994, I applied for Aunt Ada's Birth Certificate - 2ndQtr 1882 (5c 645).
