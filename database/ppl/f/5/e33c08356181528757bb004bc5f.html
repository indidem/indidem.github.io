Narrative

SMH - SHETTLE - 5 Jan 1935, at her residence,82 Barcom Avenue, Darlinghurst, MARIA, widow of the late William Charles Shettle; dearly loved mother of Percy, Jack, Eileen, Fred & Margaret (Mrs C. Henderson), & loving grandmother of Patricia, Phyllis, William, James & Marie, in her 79th year. R.I.P.
FUNERAL - SHETTLE - A Requiem Mass for the Repose of the Soul of the late Mrs. MARIA SHETTLE, of 82 Barcom Ave, Darlinghurst, will be celebrated in St. Canice's Church, Roslyn Gardens, This day Monday Morning, at 9 o'clock. R.I.P.
FUNERAL - SHETTLE - The Rels & Friends of the late Mrs. MARIA SHETTLE, of Barcom Ave, Darlinghurst, Widow of the late William Charles Shettle, are kindly invited to attend her Funeral, to leave St. Canice's Church, Roslyn Gardens, This Monday, at 2.15 pm, for Waverley Cemetery. Lot 44595.
GRANDCHILDREN - Patricia, Phyllis, William, James & Marie.
