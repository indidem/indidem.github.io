Dawson, Selwyn Ellis

Birth Name Dawson, Selwyn Ellis
Gender male
Age at Death 82 years, 3 months, 19 days

Narrative

WW2 Nominal Roll - Sapper Selwyn Dawson # NX14595 - Enlisted on 20 May 1940 at Concord, he was Discharged on 11 Dec.1945, 2/6 Field Company. He saw service in the Middle East, he was then on the Oronia/Oronsay on his was back to either Australia, or the war with Japan when the boat was torpedoed & they were picked up by the Japanese & spent the rest of the war in Changi Prisoner of War Camp.
Selwyn was 82 years old when he died on 14 Mar 2001, at Ettalong formally of Yagoona West. The death notice was in the Telegraph.
