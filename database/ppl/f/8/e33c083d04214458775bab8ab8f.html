Narrative

I have found this death notice in the Parish Records of Narrellan. Most of the deaths are in the County of Argyle buried between July & Nov 1827. It says James Nye from H.McArthur Esq., from District of Cook, was Buried on the 24th Oct 1827, abt 60 years old. Under ships name it says what looks like ?? "1818" he was a Labourer, & the death was signed by Rev Thomas Hassall, when all the other enteries were signed by Richard Hill, Schoolmaster of Bong Bong. Entry #1827- 457
