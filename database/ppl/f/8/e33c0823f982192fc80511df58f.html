SMH - Awfully Sudden Death - It was reported yesterday morning at the Darlinghurst Police Station that a man named Thomas Cook had been found dead in bed. On Thursday night, it appears, the deceased retired to bed with his wife about 11pm, when he was in good health. About 2am next morning she awoke up & found him dead. Dr. Mackellar was sent for, and he gave it as his opinion that death had resulted from decease of the heart. The deceased was a commission agent, residing at Christiana Terrace, Fitzror St. Surrey Hills, & was 61yrs old. An inquest was held at 11am to-day.
Inquest # 475 - 27 May 1876 at Sydney. Thomas Cook:- Before H. Shiell, Finding - Natural Causes.
FUNERAL - The Friends of the late THOMAS COOK, Broker, are invited to attend his Funeral, This Saturday afternoon 27 May, 1876, to move from his late residence, Christiana Terrace, Fitzroy St, near Crown St, Surry Hills, at half-past 2 o'clock, for the Necropolis, Rookwood.
