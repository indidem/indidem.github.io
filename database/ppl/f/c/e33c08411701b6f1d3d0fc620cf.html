Narrative

SMH - DEAN - HERBERT WALTER - 21 Jan, 1948, at his residence, 121 Gibbs St, Rockdale (former Inspector of Police) dearly loved husband of Ada, dear father of Dorothea (Mrs.Mears), Mildred (Mrs.Lloyd), Lillian (Mrs.Binskin), Walter, Thelma (Mrs.Sparkes), & Roy, & stepfather of Les, Jim(dec),Mona (Mrs.Jeffrey), Norman, Dulcie (Mrs.Peadon) & Harry, aged 69 years.
FUNERAL - DEAN - The Rels & Friends of Mrs. Ada Dean, of 121 Gibbs St, Rockdale, Mr.& Mrs.G.Mears, Mr.& Mrs.A.Lloyd, Mr.& Mrs. F.Binskin, Mr.& Mrs.W.Dean, Mr.& Mrs.J.Sparkes, Mr.& Mrs.R.Dean, Mr.& Mrs.L.Dent, Mr.& Mrs. Jeffrey, Mr.& Mrs. N.Dent, Mrs.D.Peadon, Mr.H.Dent, & their Families, are invited to attend the Funewral of her dearly loved Husband & Father & Stepfather, & Grandfather, HERBERT WALTER DEAN (formally Inspector of Police), which will leave St. John's CofE, Pacific Highway, Rockdale, this Thursday, after a service at 2.30pm, for the Church of England Cemetery, Woronora.
