Atkin, Enoch Painter

Narrative

Enoch ATKIN (2nd) was a painter, born 1866 in Hull Yorkshire. He was a Methodist. He died suddenly in 1925 aged 59yrs of Pulmonary Haemorrage. He married Amelia Burke in 1889, who was a relative of Robert O'Hara Burke the explorer.
SMH - ATKIN - November 21, 1925, at his residence, Shrublands, Katoomba St, Katoomba, Enoch, beloved husband of Amelia Atkin, & father of Alan & Elsie, aged 59 years. (suddenly)
FUNERAL - The Funeral of the late Mr. Enoch ATKIN will leave his residence, Shrublands, Katoomba St, Katoomba, this Monday morning at 10 o'clock, for the Methodist Cemetery, Katoomba.
