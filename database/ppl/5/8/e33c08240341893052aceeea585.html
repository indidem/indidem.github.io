Narrative

SMH - LEWIS - Leslie Edward (Les) Jan 8, 1999, peacefully at hospital, late of Miranda, much loved husband of Eva, loving father & stepfather of Georgina, Barbara & Cliff, cherished grandfather & great-grandfather, dear brother of Norman & Stanley (deceased). Aged 87 years.
FUNERAL - Les's family & friends are kindly invited to attend his funeral service, to be conducted on Wed 13 Jan, 1999 in the South Chapel of Woronora Crematorium, Linden St, Sutherland at 11.30am. Ashes at - Centenary Courtyard 3, Lot 246.
Warrant Officer #205909 of the 24th Squadron, RAAF in 2nd WW, he Enlisted on 16/10/1939 - (28yrs) He was discharged on 17/10/1945, #3 Aircraft Depot, Laverton Victoria.
Worked for International Harvesters. They lived in Kiora Rd, Miranda.
Second Marriage to Eva, Stepchildren - Barbara & Clifford Raatz.
