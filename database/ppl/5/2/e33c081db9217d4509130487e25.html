Granleese, Mary Peisley (Widow)

Birth Name Granleese, Mary Peisley (Widow)
Gender female
Age at Death 77 years, 8 months, 10 days

Narrative

Mary Peisley, daughter of Francis J.Preisley & Mary Ann Hill, was born in Parramatta NSW, 6/10/1836, & was Baptised at St John's Parramatta, by Rev H.H.Bobart.
Mary married William Granleese in 1858 Parramatta NSW #2402, they had six children. Mary died at her daughters place, Mrs Elizabeth Ashton, Lennox St, Richmond, & is buried in St.Peter's CofE Cemetery, Richmond NSW, aged 79 years.
