Cuzner, Anne Matilda

Birth Name Cuzner, Anne Matilda
Gender female
Age at Death 89 years, 1 month, 26 days

Narrative

Annie was the daughter of James Cuzner & Eliza "Lizzie" McCarthney, born in 1865 in Shelleys Flat near Goulburn NSW, it is said she was raised by her 2 brothers after her parents died when she was young. They owned the Terminus Hotel in Cootamundra. She married in 1886 in Cootamundra NSW, & they moved to Victoria. Annie worked as a Postmistress in Baarmutha, Beechworth from 1926 to 1945, she died in 1954 in Beechworth & is buried CofE Section F, grave 121, Beechworth Cemetery.
