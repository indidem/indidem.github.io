Narrative

On 1881 Census, I have an address of Shedule 24 Woodbank Place, Manningham-
Frances Ackroyd, Head, 63yrs, Widow from Colnebrook Yorks.
Charles " son 26yrs, single, clerk, born Bradford Yks.
Harriett Ellis dau 21yrs, married " " "
George E. Ellis, son-in-law,23yrs, " carpenter, born Sunderland Durham.
Frank " G.son, 4mths, born Shipley, Bradford, Yks.
Great Uncle George Ellis arrived in Australia after visiting South Africa, on the S.S.Precurseurs (French) on 21/6/1883, with a group of men which included Grandfather John Dawson. They worked to-gether building the Queen Victoria Building in George St, Sydney, & other places before they started their own building company. The lived in Carlyle & Dean Sts, Enfield.
20/4/1982 - applied for the Death Cert for George Ellis, he died in Prince Alfred Hospital, Camperdown, late of Marrickville, 70 years old. The informant was Annie Pratt, daughter, of 141 Warren Rd, Marrickville. There were four children alive, Frank, Annie, George & Stanley, & two deceased, Harry & Wally. Also Annie hadn't married Albert Wilson by April 1928.
SMH - ELLIS - 22 April, 1928, at Royal Prince Alfred Hospital, George Edward Ellis, of Warren Rd, Marrickville, aged 70 years. At Rest. Couldn't find a Funeral Notice.
