Narrative

SMH - REED, Patricia Mary (nee Shettle) Nov 22, 1998. At Hospital, late of East Lakes. Dearly loved wife of Frank (dec), much loved mother & mother-in-law of John & Diane, David & Mark & nanna of Justine, Katherine, Timothy & Carolyn. Aged 82 years. May she rest in peace.
A Requiem Mass for the repose of Pattie"s soul will be concelebrated at St Teresa's Catholic Church, Sutherland St, Mascot next Thursday (26 Nov, 1998), appointed to commence at 1.30 pm.
At the conclusion of the prayers following Mass, the cortege will proceed to the family grave at Waverley Cemetery.
Four Grandchildren - Justine, Katherine, Timothy & Carolyn.
