Bongers, Ralph Lincoln

Birth Name Bongers, Ralph Lincoln
Gender male

Narrative

Address:- 103 Constitution Rd, Windsor Qld 4030.
Ralph enlisted for WWII on 8/9/1942, at Hill End, Qld, Corporal in lines of communication signals.
He was discharged 5/1/1944. After the war he was an Engineer for Radio Australia.
Nov 1991 - Letter from Mrs.A.Rogers to say there was a Notice in the Courier Mail in 1990 to say - Diamond Wedding Anniversary of Ralph & Annie (Sommerville) Bongers, married on 6 Dec 1930.
We met Ralph & Annie at Clive Bongers place when we went to Brisbane in 1986? He said he was in the Coast Watchers during the War. He was interested that Keith had gone to Marconi School of Radio.
