Narrative

occ railway clerk. Enlisted 1914/1915, served overseas & returned to action 1915 from Egypt. Re-enlisted.
Sgt Major James Gray sailed for England with Railway Unit, 7/2/1917 & discharged 29/1/1920.
Mentioned in despatches by Gen. Sir Douglas Haig in 16 Mar 1919. May have returned to Paris in the 1920's ?? Died at 140 Dumaresq St, Hamilton. aged 71 years.
