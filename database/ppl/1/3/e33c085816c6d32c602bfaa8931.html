Narrative

SMH - 1st August, 1927, at Royal South Sydney Hospital, Zetland, EDITH MAY SHUTTLEWORTH, dear daughter of Mr. & Mrs. Walter Shuttleworth, & loved sister of George, Leonard & Daphne, aged 32 years.
The funeral of Edith May Shuttleworthwill leave her late residence, Melrose St, Mascot, this Wednesday at 2.30pm, for Independant Cemetery, Botany.
The Members of the Mascot Congregational Church Choir are kindly invited to attend the funeral of their late Member, EDITH MAY SHUTTLEWORTH, to lrave her home in Melrose St, Mascot, this Wednesday at 2.30pm for Botany Cemetery.
Edith is buried next to her mother (General F M B - Grave 200)
