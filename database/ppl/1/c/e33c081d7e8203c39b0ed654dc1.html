Narrative

Frazer was named after his Great grandmother, Georgina Frazer.
5/5/2008 - Susan has found a site for Brisbane Cemeteries. Frazer Bongers died in 1983 not 1985, aged 93yrs, he is buried with his wife Gertrude in the Mt. Gravatt Cemetery, 582 Mains Rd., MacGregor, Brisbane, Lawn Section, Portion L7, Grave #313.
His daughter, Joan Bongers was a Spinster. For many years she lived in Waverton, Sydney, then she moved up to Brisbane for a couple of years, then moved back toMosman. I found her death on D.P.S.
