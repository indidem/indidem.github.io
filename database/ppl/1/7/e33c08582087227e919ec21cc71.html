Narrative

SMH -- Rose SHUTTLEWORTH, 17 January, 1945, at her residence, 33 Roslyn St, Mascot, dear wife of Leonard, & mother of Leonard, Beryl, Walter & Keith, aged 46 years.
The relatives & friends of Mr. Leonard Shuttleworth & family are invited to attend the funeral of ROSE HANNAH MAY SHUTTLEWORTH, to leave her late residence this afternoon, for the Congregational Cemetery, Botany.
The relatives & friends of Mr.R.Brown, Mrs.Lewis(dec), Mr.W.Brown, Mr.A.Brown, Mr.Eric Brown, Miss F.Brown, Mrs.G.Graham, Mr.W. & Mr.G.Shuttleworth, Mr.R.Holmes, & their families, & Mrs.Pritchard & Mrs.Woolley, are invited to the funeral of their dear daughter, daughter-in-law, sister, sister-in-law, & dear friend, ROSE HANNAH MAY SHUTTLEWORTH.
