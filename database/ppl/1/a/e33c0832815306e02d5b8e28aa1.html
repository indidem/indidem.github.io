Narrative

Roxhannah was christened at West Maitland Wesleyan Methodist Church.
SMH - Roxana died on 14 Oct. 1923 at her son's residence, "Arcacia" Garnett St, Dulwich Hill (Hurlstone Park), dearly loved wife of G.F.Noad snr. 70 years old.
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of Mr.G.F.Noad, Sen., late of Pyrmont, are invited to attend the Funeral of his late dearly beloved wife, ROXANA; to leave her son's residence, Acacia, Garnet St, Hurlstone Park this afternoon, at 1.15 o'clock, for the Methodist Cemetery, Rookwood (old portion), via Lewisham Station.
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of Mr.& Mrs.G.F.Noad, jnr., Mr.& Mrs.J.J.Noad, Mr.& Mrs.H.Noad, Mr.& Mrs.S.T.Noad are invited to attend the Funeral of their late dearly beloved Mother, ROXANA NOAD; to leave Acadia, Garnet St., Hurlstone Park, this afternoon, at 1.15 o'clock, for the Methodist Cemetery, Rookwood (old section), via Lewisham station.
Roxana was buried in Old Wesleyan Cemetery at Rookwood with her parents, James & Maria LLoyd. Also a baby Maria Noad, died 19 May 1886, #35406 Plot 998-1002.
