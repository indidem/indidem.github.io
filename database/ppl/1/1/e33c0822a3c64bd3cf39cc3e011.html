Narrative

George Ellis was born on 9 July, 1889 at Carlisle St, Enfield.
WW2 Nominal Rolls - Service # NX44901, Enlisted 16 Sep 1941 at Concord, Discharged 11 Jan 1946 as Craftsman, 5th Aust Mechanical Equip W/Ships. I have a photo with George & family taken 1940.
SMH - ELLIS - George Edward - 1 December, 1954, at Masonic Hospital, Ashfield, of 159 Major's Bay Rd, Concord, dearly loved husband of Violet, loving father of George, Irene (Mrs.Dubos), Jack & Norman & loving brother of Mrs.Wilson (Jenny), aged 65yrs.
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of Mrs. Violet Ellis & Family, of Concord, are invited to attend the Funeral of her dearly loved Husband & their dear Father GEORGE EDWARD ELLIS; to leave St. Paul's C of E, Burwood, this Friday (3/12/1954) after service at 10.45am, for Rookwood Crematorium.
ELLIS - Pride of Burwood Lodge, No.39, I.O.O.F. - Officers & Members of the above Lodge are invited to attend the Funeral of their late esteemed Bro. GEORGE E. ELLIS N.G. see family notice.
George & Vi's Ashes are in the Wall of Rememberance at St. Paul's CofE Burwood.
