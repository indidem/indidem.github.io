Dargin, Emily Maria

Birth Name Dargin, Emily Maria
Gender female
Age at Death 74 years, 9 months, 17 days

Narrative

Emily's parents were James Dargin & Sophia Ezzey, married in 1824, at her Baptism her father was a Grazier at Richmond Rd, Windsor, they had eight children. Emily & William were baptised at St Matthews Windsor 21 days apart. 1842/981 Vol 26a.
SMH - FORRESTER; 1st July, 1917 at Warwick, Homebush; EMILY, widow of the late William Forrester of Warwick Farm, Liverpool.
FUNERAL - FORRESTER - The Funeral of the late Mrs. EMILY FORRESTER will leave her late residence, Warwick, Abbotsford Rd, Homebush, This Day, Monday, at 12.45 for Strathfield Station, thence by rail to Liverpool station, for St. Luke's Cemetery, by train leaving Sydney 1.15pm.
