Narrative

BAPTISM - John Thomas Irish, was baptised at St James CofE, Sydney, by Rev F.D.Priddle, son of Charles & Martha Irish, his father was a Tailor, & they lived in Hoskings Buildings, Castlereagh St Sydney,
SMH - Death - IRISH -July 18th, 1923, at his residence, "Glenholme" Broughton Rd, Homebush. John Thomas Derriman Irish, beloved husband of Ann R. Irish, aged 71yrs. By request, no flowers.
SMH - IRISH - The relatives & friends of Mrs. J.T.D.Irish are invited to attend the Funeral of her beloved husband, JOHN THOMAS DERRIMAN IRISH; to leave his late residence, "Glenholme" Broughton Rd, Homebush, this Thursday, at 2.30pm, for the Church of England Cemetery, Rookwood, by road. Sydney friends join the funeral train leaving Mortuary Station, Regent St, City, at 1.55pm, & alight at #4 Mortuary Station. By request, no flowers.
SMH - IRISH - The relatives & friends of John C., Thomas W., Walter G., Arthur E., Charles A., Albert S., Robert G., and Mrs James Irish, are invited to the Funeral of their dearly beloved Father -JOHN THOMAS DERRIMAN IRISH, to leave "Glenholme" etc etc....
PPS - The Death & Funeral notice in the SMH has recorded his name as Berriman !!!
Rookwood Anglican Section 7, Row 21, Plot 948.
