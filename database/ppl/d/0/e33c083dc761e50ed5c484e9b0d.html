Narrative

I met the Rowe family at the Collingwood Re-union in 1992. I didn't know that George Taylor Rowe had died at Collingwood in 1859.!!! He had been Hon. Secretary of the A.J.C., and his son George Sydney Taylor Rowe Jnr. become Secretary of the A.J.C
SMH - ROWE, ROBERT CLYDE - 10 July, 1991, late of Cronulla & formally of Liverpool (NX 28184), dearly beloved husband of Joyce, loving father & father-in-law of Jennifer & Alister, Elizabeth & Ken, much loved Bob-Bob of Elizabeth, Peter, David, Anthony, Michael, Jane & Richard, dearly loved great-grandfather of Alice & Robert, aged 85 years, forever in our hearts.
Robert is buried in Liverpool Anglican Cemetery Sec F1 lot 1 together with his little son Robert Edward Clyde Rowe, who died on 16/11/1935 one day old.
