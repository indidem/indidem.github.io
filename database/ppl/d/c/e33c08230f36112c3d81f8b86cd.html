Narrative

SMH - GRIST - 18 January, 1915, at her residence, 4 High St, Waverley, MARY ELIZABETH GRIST, dearly loved wife of John Grist, aged 62 years. Somerset (England) papers please copy.
FUNERAL - The friends of Mr. John Grist & Family are invited to attend the Funeral of their beloved wife & mother; to leave her residence, 4 High St, Waverley, this day, Tuesday, at 2.45pm, for Waverley Cemetery.
