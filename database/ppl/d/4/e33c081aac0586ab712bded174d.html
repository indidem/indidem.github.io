Narrative

John Gray was a Shoemaker of Church St, Parramatta.
I have had the help of Peter & Reg Proctor to compile most of these records.
I also found & checked some of these dates in the Parish Records of St Johns CofE Church Parramatta & All Saints CofE Church in North Parramatta at the Parramatta Library.
John died at his daughter, Ruth Bongers place, in Leichhardt.
