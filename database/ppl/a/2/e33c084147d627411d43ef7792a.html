SMH - PIGGOTT - 18 August, 1939, at 61 Cross St., Guildford, ALFRED (Bert) PIGGOTT, dearly loved husband of Louise Piggott, & loving father of Rita (Mrs.Faulkner), Della (Mrs.Rutherford), Marjorie (Mrs.Terry), & Ronald. (Son of the late Charles Piggott of Minto), aged 59 years.
