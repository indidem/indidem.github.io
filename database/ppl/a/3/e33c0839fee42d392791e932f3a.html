SMH - WENHOLZ - 22 March, 1904, George Henry Wenholz, after a long & painful illness, in his 54th year of his age. May his soul rest in peace. German papers please copy.
FUNERAL - The funeral of the late Mr. GEORGE H. WENHOLZ will take place at Church of England Cemetery, Rookwood this afternoon, at 3 o'clock. Funeral train leaves Mortuary Station, Sydney, at 2.23pm.
SMH - WENHOLZ - 1st September, 1922, at Prince Alfred Hospital, George Wenholz, of Palice St, Petersham, late retired school teacher, of West Maitland, Leichhardt, Camperdown & Waterloo. age 67 years.
ROOKWOOD CEMETERY - Methodist, Lutheran & Ukranian Orthodox Section. This George Wenholz is buried with Clara d 6/12/19 13, age55yrs, Eustace d17/4/1914, age 29yrs, & Ferdinand, d13/8/1933, 41yrs. in grave 146754. I found them in Rookwood Cemetery records.
