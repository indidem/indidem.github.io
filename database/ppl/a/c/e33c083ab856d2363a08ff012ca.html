Narrative

Herbert was the son of Frederick & Eliza Yabsley. His probate was 9/10/1935, West Maitland.?
SMH -- YABSLEY -- 9 October, 1935, at a private hospital, Sydney, HERBERT WILLIAM MYLER, third son of the late Frederick & Eliza Yabsley, of Ashfield, & loving brother of Frederick, Oak St, Ashfield, Arthur J.R. (dec), Killara), & Irene of Leura, aged 54 years.
FUNERAL -- of the late HERBERT W.M.YABSLEY, late Headmaster Of Junior Technical School, West Maitland, will leave our Chapel at Redfern, this morning, after a short service at 19o'clock, for Rookwood Crematorium.
The Masonic Lodge Sutherland, No.585 U.G.L. N.S.W., The Officers & Members of the above Lodge are requested to attend the funeral of their late Brother, HERBERT WILLIAM M. YABSLEY, see family notice.
The Members of the N.S.W. Masocic Club are fraternally invited to attend the Funeral of the late Bro. H.W.YABSLEY, leaving our Parlours, at Redfern, to-day, at 10am, for Rookwood Crematorium.
