Narrative

Born abt 1820/1821 in Limpley Stoke, Bradford on Avon, Wiltshire. I haven't been able to find a Christening yet.
1/5/1990 Found marriage of Charles Grist & Caroline Elizabeth Morgan in Bristol, 3rd Qtr 1842,(X1 153). Photocopied marriage at St.Marys, CofE, Redcliffe, Bristol on 25 July, 1990.
20/7/2001 Found Charles & Caroline Grist on the 1851 Census in the little village of Limpley Stoke, near Bradford on Avon, Wiltshire. They had 3 children - Sarah, Julia & John.
Aug/2001 Applied for Parish Records for Limpley Stoke in Bradford but couldn't find any Grists there.
Sep/2001 Found the Grist family on the 1861 Census in Upper Limpley Stoke. Charles was 3yrs old! born 1858. Sarah not there! Still haven't been able to find them on St. Catherines yet.
Dec 2001 Looked thru 72 Non-Conformist Churches in Wiltshire but wasn't able to find them! Where are they!
