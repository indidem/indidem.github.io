Narrative

John & Helen Harvey were married on 23rd October, 1829, in the Parish Church in Inverness, Scotland. John was a Cooper, they lived in Huntley St, Inverness. John's father was Alexander Fraser.
I found the marriage & all the childrens births in the Parish Registers. I don't know when John & Helen died, but they had died before Eliza & Georgina came to Australia in 1854.
