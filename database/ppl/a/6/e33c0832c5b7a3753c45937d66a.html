Narrative

Edward Cruwys was born in 1914 at "Irene" 5 Barr St Balmain. He married Bessie Chidgey in 1941 & had 3 children. He lived at Massey St, Gladesville. Gurney St, Villawood, Landsdowne Rd, Lansdowne, then moved to Victoria St, Taree, where he died in 1994.
SMH - CRUWYS, Edward Dudley - 11 Aug 1944, late of Jacaranda Ave, Taree, beloved husband of Bessie (dec), loving father & father-in-law of Patricia & Gilbert, Barry & Maureen, Ralph & Betty, loving grandfather of their families. 79 years.

"My Paternal Grandfather was a Painter & Decorator in Balmain. & my Grandmother had a Shirt Factory & all her daughters worked in the factory. My mother was put in charge when she was 14 years old, & when she married & had us two boys, she then opened her own shop in Rozelle, dressmaking."
Memories of Ebb Cruwys - March 1988.
