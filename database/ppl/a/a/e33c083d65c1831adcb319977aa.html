Narrative

SMH - PRINCE - 1st October, 1931, at his residence, Northumberland St, Liverpool, GEORGE, dearly loved husband of Eva Prince, & loving father of Tom & Jack.
PRINCE - 1/10/1931, at his residence Liverpool, GEORGE ROBERT, dearly loved son of Mr.& Mrs. G.Prince, of Livingstone Rd, Marrickville, & loving brother of Beattie (Mrs.D.Munro), of Auburn, Mrs.M.Prince, Mr.& Mrs.Fred Prince, of Marrickville, aged 56 years.
PRINCE - 1/10/1931, at his residence, Liverpool, GEORGE ROBERT PRINCE, brother-in-law of Edward, Leslie, Tom, & Cecil Ashcroft, aged 56 years.
FUNERAL - The Rels & friends of Mrs. Eva Prince & sons Tom & Jack, are invited to attend the funeral of her beloved husband & their father- GEORGE ROBERT PRINCE, to leave St Luke's Church Liverpool this Friday, after the service at 3pm, for New CofE Cemetery, Liverpool. Sec F1 lot 20/1.
Also 6 other Funeral Notices from - Masonic Lodge Liverpool #197, where he was the Treasurer, signed by R. Clyde-Rowe, Secretary; Liverpool R.A.Chapter #66 Supreme Grand Chapter NSW; Guildford Mark Lodge #85, Grand Lodge M.M.M.,NSW; The Supreme Grand Chapter of the Royal Arch Masons of NSW; St Andrew R.A. Chapter #15, NSW Constitution, where his father was the Secretary; & St Andrews Mark Lodge #25, where his father was also the Secretary. All the adds said the service would be at 3 o'clock at St Luke's Church, then the Cortege will proceed to the new Cemetery at Liverpool. Regalia at the grave.
