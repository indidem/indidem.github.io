Narrative

SMH -- Harriett Ann Stores died 4 Nov, 1943, at Marrickville, dearly loved wife of James (Jim) Stores, of River St, Earlwood, mother of Doris (Mrs.Handley), & Florence, Eva, & George (all deceased), aged 78 years.
The Family & friends of Mr.James (Jim) Stores, of 80 River Street, Earlwood, also Mr.& Mrs.S.Parkes & Mrs.D.Handley & families, are invited to the funeral of their dear wife, & their mother & grandmother, HARRIETT ANN STORES, to leave our Chapel this afternoon, after a short service, for the Methodist Cemetery, Woronora.
