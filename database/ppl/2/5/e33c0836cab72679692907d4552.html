Narrative

6/6/2005 - can't find any dates in Probate for Mary or August Haack or Hanck or Hauck.
I Can't find them in Auburn or Granville Electoral Rolls for 1925 for any of above names, and I can't find any children for them. Will have to apply for Mary's death in 1943 Auburn #25451.

Aug 2005 - applied to Joy Murrin for Mary Haacks death Transcription. She died on 13 Oct, 1943, at Newington State Hospital, we call it Silverwater nowdays. She was 68 years old, an old age pensioner, & she died of an Aneurism of the Aorta. It says her fathers name was James McFadden a "Contractor" & her mother Catherine Shields. The Informant was R.G.McIntosh, Chief Clerk of Newington State Hospital. (No Mention of her Husband, maybe he had died)! It says she was born at Bathurst, married at 29yrs to August John Haack, at Redfern, Sydney and there was no Children, & she was buried in the Catholic Cemetery at Rookwood, but I couldn't find her there. Now I'll have to look in SMH.

SMH - The Relatives & Friends of Mr.A.J.Haack & John, are kindly invited to attend the Funeral of his dearly beloved wife & his dear mother, MARY; to leave St. Joachim's Church, Lidcombe, this afternoon, Thursday (14/10/43) at 2.30pm, for the Catholic Cemetery, Rookwood. (Labor Funerals)
Can't find a Death Notice.
