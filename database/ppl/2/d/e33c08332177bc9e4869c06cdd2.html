Narrative

Ruby Ormsby has given me a copy of the Marriage Transcription for Charlie Mills & Alice Ellen Shettle on the 23 June, 1899 at 471 Pitt St, Sydney by the Independant Presbyterian Church by Rev George Hay. Charlie was 19yrs old & lived at 65 Carlisle St, Leichhardt. The Father of the Bridegroom gave his consent because the Groom was under 21 years. Alice Ellen was 18yrs old & lived at 7 Carlisle St, Leichhardt. The Father of the Bride gave his consent as the Bride was under 21 years.

SMH - FUNERAL - The Rel & Friends of late CHARLIE MILLS, of Marrickville, are invited to attend his funeral; to leave our Funeral Home, 15 Norton St, Leichhardt, this morning, after service at 9.15am for Methodist Cemetery, Rookwood. Indepentant lot 173073. Labour Funerals.
