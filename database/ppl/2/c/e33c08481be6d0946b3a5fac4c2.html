Narrative

SMH - LANGTRY - Isabel Adelaide - 7 Jun 1970, at a private hospital, of 102 Grand Parade, Brighton-le-Sands, dearly loved wife of the late George Langtry, loved mother of Colin & Geoffrey, dear mother-in- law of Louise & Ivy, fond grandmother & great-grandmother of their children, & dear sister of Dorothy, Alf & Colin, in her 80th year.
