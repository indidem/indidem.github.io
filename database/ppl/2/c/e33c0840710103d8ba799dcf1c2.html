SMH - DANIELS - 25 September, 1927, Alice Eliza Daniels, of Boronia, 13 Albert Rd, Strathfield, relict of the late Franklin Daniels, & loved mother of Dorothy (Mrs.H.Grenenger) & Ronald, aged 54 years.
DANIELS - The rels & friends of Mr.R.Daniels & Mr.& Mrs.H.Grenenger, are invited to attend the funeral of their late Mother, ALICE ELIZA DANIELS; to leave her late residence, Boronia, 13 Albert Rd, Strathfield, this Monday, at 2.45pm, for CofE Cemetery, Rookwood, by road. Motor funeral. Friends per 1.55pm funeral train, please alight at No.4 platform in cemetery.
DANIELS - Mrs. Alice Amelia, - The rels & friends of Mr. John Wright, of 20 Roberts St, Strathfield, & the late Elizabeth Wright & families, are kindly invited to the funeral of their beloved Daughter, Sister & Sister-in-law, ALICE AMELIA DANIEL. The motor funeral will leave Boronia, etc etc....
