Narrative

Marriage Certificate - I Thomas Cook, member of Church of England Church, and I Rosey McFedden,(blurred) member of the Independent Church, and I Josh Beazley of Pitt St. Redfern, Sydney, Minister of Congregational Church, do hereby Certify, that Thomas Cook, of George St, Sydney, and Rosa McFedden of Redfern St, Redfern, were joined to-gether in Wedlock by me on the eleventh day of February, 1852, at Redfern Congregational Church, in the presence of James McFedden - Redfern St, Redfern, and George Watson of Sussex St, Sydney. signed Josh Beazley, Minister.
P.S. I have been to Redfern Cong. Church many times in the 1940's & 50's. Joyce Dive was the Mission Sister there. The Annual Missionary Division Displays were held there a couple of times.
Rosa died 14 May, 1911 at Queen St, Woollahra. She is buried at Waverley Cemetery Sec 21, lot 164
