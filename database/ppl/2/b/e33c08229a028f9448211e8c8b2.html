Narrative

There is a Christening on the I.G.I. for a Thomas Ellis on 21/7/1833 at Wibsey, Yorkshire Eng, father Edward, mother Elizabeth ?? maybe the wrong one !!
16/8/1984 - applied for the Marriage Cert for Thomas Ellis & Jane Elwin - his father was Edward Ellis, Land & Coal Agent; her father was Joseph Elwen, husbandman.
16/8/1984 - applied for the birth Cert for Anne Ellis on 24 Oct 1861, Mum said she thought she was born on the 30th or 31st October, my mum told me her mother told her her mother had died & she was brought up by her grandmother.
8/7/1985 - applied for the Death Cert for Thomas Ellis (like the photo I have). he died at 2 Fraser Terrace, Manningham, Bradford, 62 years old. Found out his 2nd wifes name was Elizabeth Robinson.
