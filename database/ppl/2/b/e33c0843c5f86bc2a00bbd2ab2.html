Narrative

"Jack" Pund was born 1863 in Three Mile Mine, Stanley Vic. The Electoral rolls in 1914 shown Jack as residing at Baarmutha, Beechworth & emloyed as a Mine Manager. He worked as a farmer in 1923. The Electoral rolls of 1924 & 1936, again show Jack as residing at Baarmutha, Beechworth and employed as a Mine manager, but he was working as a grazier when he died in 1947, & he was again residing at Three Mile. He is buried in the Church of England Section F, grave 121, in Beechworth Cemetery.
