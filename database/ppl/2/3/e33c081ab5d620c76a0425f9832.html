Dunn, Margaret

Birth Name Dunn, Margaret
Gender female
Age at Death 33 years, 8 months, 25 days

Narrative

Robert & Margaret (Frater) Dunn, with their children, Margaret & Mark, arrived steerage in NSW in 1838. Her parents were pillars of the Presbyterian Church.
In the Index of Baptism (Old Records) there is a record of a Birth of Margaret Gray to John & Margaret Gray in 1857 #9612. Did they have another child ???
When Margaret died at her home at Parramatta, she was only 33 years old, she died of Phthisis, (TB). She had been married to John Gray for a few days over 10 years. She is buried in All Saints C of E Cemetery, North Parramatta with her baby daughter Alice Jessie, (1 mth old), while her husband is buried in at St. John's Cemetery, Parramatta.
