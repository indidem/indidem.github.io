Wilkinson, Lily

Birth Name Wilkinson, Lily
Gender female
Age at Death 76 years, 2 months, 1 day

Narrative

When the family left B'lick in 1912, Lily went around to all her family & friends to get an autograph in her Autograph Book. I still have that Book, with signatures & addresses, especially from the family. Lily died in Auburn Hospital, aged 76 years.
