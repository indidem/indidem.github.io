Grist, Norman Henry

Birth Name Grist, Norman Henry

Age at Death 72 years

Narrative

Dad was born at 76 Queen St, Woollahra. He died at "Sirrocca" Boorooma Place, Cronulla.
My father Norman worked for the AMP Society all his life. My brother Bruce reconds he was chief Accountant, & for about the last 10yrs he was Property Manager, He also was a Director of St.George Building Society before it became a Bank.
They lived at Rocky Point Rd, Kogarah for about 10yrs, then moved to Newton Road, Strathfield.
After Dad died Mum moved into a little Villa in South Hurstville
SMH - GRIST - Norman Henry, 15 January 1972, late of Strathfield & Cronulla, husband of Frances, father of Shirley, Bruce & Margaret, father-in-law of Keith, Jan & John, & Grandfather of their children. Privately cremated 18 January, 1972. Section 11 KK.
