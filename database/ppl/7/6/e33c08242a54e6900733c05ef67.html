Narrative

Lily Menzies was Don Bradmans sister-in-law. My brother Bruce can remember Dad taking him to a Cricket Match & afterwards meeting "The Don".
Uncle Frank & Aunty Lily Underwood lived in Strathfield for a while when they were first married. I have a Photo taken outside Strathfield Homebush Congregational Church where Janice, my sister Margaret & myself went to Sunday School together. I have done some of the Menzies Family History.
