Narrative

SMH - THORNTON, Willie Andrew, 27 October, 1969, at Albury Base Hospital, beloved husband of the late Lucy Jane Thornton, of Albury, & late of 31 Birdwood Ave, Ryde; loved father of Thelma (Mrs.Clark, Sydney), Silvester (dec), Trevor (dec), Ivy (Mrs.Barnsdale of Albury), fond grandfather of Kevin Clark, Denise Barnsdale, Judith & Karen Thornton, & great-grandfather of Karen & Judith Clarke, in his 91st yr.
FUNERAL - The relatives of the late WILLIE ANDREW THORNTON are invited to attend his Funeral; to leave St. Anne's Church of England Church, Church St, Ryde, to-morrow morning, after a service at 11o'clock, for Northern Suburbs Crematorium.
