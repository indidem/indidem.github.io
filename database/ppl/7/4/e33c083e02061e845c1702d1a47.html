Narrative

WW2 Nominal Roll - R.A.N.
Rupert Robison was born in Springwood in 1909, He enlisted in the Navy on 1 Jan 1923, in Sydney, his wife's name was Betty. He was Leiutenant/Captain on the H.M.A.S. Voyager when it ran aground in Timor. He was Discharged on 20 August 1944, as a Lieutenant-Commander, he was at HMAS Penguin when discharged, & he received the Distinguished Service Cross.
SMH - ROBINSON, Rupert Cowper, DSC, (Former Lieutenant Commander, Royal Australian Navy) - Dearly loved husband of Elizabeth (dec), loved father of Richard & loving uncle of his nieces & nephews. Aged 90years. Rest in peace.
Relatives & friends of Mr RUPERT COWPER ROBINSON DSC are advised that his funeral will be held on Thursday (10 June, 1999). Cremation will take place at the end of the service at 2.15pm, in a Chapel at Rookwood Crematorium.
