Cambourn, Thomas

Birth Name Cambourn, Thomas
Gender male
Age at Death unknown

Narrative

Thomas had various Occupations - he was an Apprentice Blacksmith, Engine Fitter, Shopkeeper & Master Baker.
They lived at 31 St Martins Street, Marshfield, Marlborough Eng. The bakery must have been doing well, because James & John listed as "scholars" so were still at school. Edward was listed as teacher & William as apprentice baker.
Probate - Thomas CAMBOURN of 15 Saint Martin's Marlborough Wiltshire, baker, died 13 August, 1909. Probate Salisbury, 20 September to Sarah Jane Cambourn widow & Edward James HILL retired painter & plumber. Effects 308 pounds 14s.
