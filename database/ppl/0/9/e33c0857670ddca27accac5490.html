Narrative

SMH -- JOHNS, Noah -- 22 March, 1972, at hospital, of 13 Garrish St, Gladesville, dearly loved husband of the late Mabel Johns, loved father of Alan, Daphne (Mrs.Dundon) & Muriel (Mrs. Ritchie), fond mother-in-law of George & Gordon, fond grandfather & great-grandfather of their children, aged 84 years.
The rels & friends of the late NOAH JOHNS are invited to attend his funeral, to leave Christ Church, Victoria Rd, Gladesville, to-morrow Friday morning, after a service at 9.45 o'clock, for Northern Suburbs Crematorium.
