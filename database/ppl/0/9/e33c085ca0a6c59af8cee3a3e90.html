Street, Robert John

Birth Name Street, Robert John
Gender male
Age at Death 22 years, 1 month, 12 days

Narrative

The AIF Project - Robert John Street, #2204, born 1893 Glen Innis, CofE, single aged 22yrs, Father, Snelling Street, Bristol Rd, Hurstville, Enlisted 13/4/1915 as a Private, 1st Battalion, 6th Reinforcement. Embarkation details, Unit embarked from Sydney NSW, on board HMAT A63 Karool on 16/6/1915, as a Private 1st Battalion, Killed in Action 6-9 Aug, 1915 Lone Pine, Gallipoli, date of death 7 Aug, 1915, aged 22yrs. Buried Lone Pine Cemetery, Plot 11, Row B, Grave #19, Gallipoli, Turkey, AWM #31.
Parents - Snelling Francis & Mary I. Street, "The Grange", Bristol Rd, Hurstville, NSW. Born Glen Innis NSW.
Family/military connections - Two cousins Broughton Keith Luscombe killed in action & Roy Stafford , won the VC at Passchendaele, returned to Australia.
