Narrative

SMH -- Leonard George Shuttleworth, died 2nd March, 1977, at hospital, late of Villawood, dear husband of Elsie (Peg) Shuttleworth, loved father of John, Pam, Pat & Wayne, dear father-in-law of Margaret, Chris & Terry, devoted grandfather of their children, beloved brother of Wally, Keith & Beryl, aged 56 years, in Gods care.
Leonards funeral was at St. Paul's Church of England Church, Chapel Rd, Bankstown, on Monday morning at 9am, and afterwards to Rookwood Crematorium.
Chester Hill Bowling Club directors & members are requested to attend the funeral of their late esteemed member.
