Aug 2005 - Applied to Joy Murrin for Rose's Death Transcription. She died on 6 Nov 1936 at Eulomogo, Talbragar Shire. Her name was Roseanna Regensburger, she was 73 years old, she died of Cachexia, Carcinoma of the Breast, Secondary Stomach. Her father was James McFadden, a Farmer, & her mother was Catherine Shields. The Informant was her son - Augustus Regensburger of Eulomogo. It says she was born at Bathurst, married at 22yrs to August Regensburger at Parramatta. Her children were Catherine 49, Percy 47, Augustus 44, Carl 36, living, 2 females deceased. (Elsie & Hortense)

I can't find a Death or Funeral Notice for her in SMH.
