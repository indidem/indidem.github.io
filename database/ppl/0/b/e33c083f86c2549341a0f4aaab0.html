Braithwaite, Edward

Birth Name Braithwaite, Edward
Gender male
Age at Death 73 years, 7 months, 29 days

Narrative

Edward & Margaret lived at Exeter Farm, he was a leading horse breeder; he bred Clydesdales, thoroughbreds, hacks, hunters & ponies. He had a great love for animals & skill in handling them. His neighbours regarded him as the local vet & called him whenever they had sick animals. He was a keen exxhibitor at all local shows & the Royal Show. He also had a fine tenor voice, singing around the piano appears to have been a regular part of his life. After the death of his 1st wife Margaret in 1885, he married her older sister, Elizabeth. (See Wendy Phillips for the full story)
