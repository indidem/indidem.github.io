Narrative

SMH - WEISS - 20 December, 1931, at the residence of his daughter (Mrs. Genders), 1 Gower St, Hurlstone Park, FREDERICK ADAM WEISS, in his 93rd year.
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of Mr.& Mrs.G.Whittaker, Mr.& Mrs.A.Weiss, Mr.& Mrs.W.Weiss, Mr.& Mrs.A.Fields, Mr.& Mrs.G.Weiss, Mr.& Mrs.A.Genders, Mr.& Mrs. L.Weiss, Mr.& Mrs.F.Weiss & Families, are invited to attend the Funeral of their late beloved father & grandfather, FREDERICK ADAM WEISS, to leave the residence of his daughter, Mrs. A.Genders of Hurlstone Park, this Monday, at 2.15pm, for the Methodist Cemetery, Rookwood.
