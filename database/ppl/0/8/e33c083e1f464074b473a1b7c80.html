Narrative

SMH - ASHCROFT - 6 May,1946 - at Parkes, & late of 7 Shepherd St, Maroubra, HENRY ASHCROFT, loved husband of the late Charlotte Ashcroft, & dear father of (Mrs.Griffin), Mark, Claude, Pearl (Mrs. White), Lance, Ward, Vera (Mrs.Bourke), Gladys (Mrs.Parker) & Harold, aged 93yrs. At rest.
FUNERAL - ASHCROFT - The rels & friends of the late HENRY ASHCROFT, late of Maroubra, are kindly invited to attend his Funeral; to leave the Presbyterian Church, Maroubra Junction, this Wednesday, after the service at 2pm, for Presbyterian Cemetery, Botany.
