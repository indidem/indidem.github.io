Pund, George William Beal

Birth Name Pund, George William Beal
Gender male
Age at Death 50 years, 10 months, 15 days

Narrative

George was born in Victoria, but he died in Nambucca District Hospital, Macksville NSW, from Cerebral thombosis & Coronary thombosis.
George worked as a Solicitor, but was later strucked off. He worked as a clerk from 1915 to 1923, when he married in 1923 he resided in Petersham NSW. The Electoral roll in 1930 shows George as residing at Carcolla vie Bellbrook NSW, where he was employed as a Farmer. He worked as a Dairy Farmer in 1947 in Kempsey then worked as a Dairy farmer in Macksville NSW. He is buried in the Roman Catholic Cemetery, Macksville in 1947.
