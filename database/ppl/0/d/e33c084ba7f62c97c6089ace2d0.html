Narrative

SMH - Simpkins, Montague Henry - 30 December, 1970, at his residence, Rose Bay, beloved husband of Henrietta, loved father of Shirley, Marjorie, Laurel, Francis & Terence & fond grandfather of their children. Requiescat in pace.
The FUNERAL of Montague Henry Simpkins will be celebrated at St. Anne's Shrine, North Bondi, to-morrow Tuesday at 9 o'clock for the Catholic Lawn Section at Woronora Cemetery. Montague was an Accountant.
