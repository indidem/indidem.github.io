Narrative

Georgina's family lived in Huntley Street, Inverness. John Fraser was a cooper, Their mothers maiden name was Helen Harvey..
Eliza Fraser, aged 21, and Georgina Fraser, aged 18, arrived per 'St Helena' on 10 Dec 1854.
Shipping records said both parents had died by 1854.
Baby George and Georgina buried at Bathurst Cemetery.
12/11/02 - Phone call from Cheryl Rolfe - 8 Gough St, Emu Plains 2750. Phone 047.351.478.
I have been looking for Eliza Frazer for nearly 20 years, Cheryl said she married Wilhem Stieme at Scots Church, Sydney; he was an Orchardist & they lived in Prospect which is not far from Parramatta. Gysbert & Mary Ann Bongers were Florists & had a shop in Church St, Parramatta.
