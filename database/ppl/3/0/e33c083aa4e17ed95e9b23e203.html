Narrative

Vera Notting & Herbert Yabsley were married at the brides parents place by the Rev Dr Henry Steele-Craik, the minister of Summer Hill Congregational Church.
Vera died just after Sheila was 5 years old. Berry said she was 14 years old when her mother died.
Their father was a school teacher, 3 of the girls were school teachers (Berry, Margaret & Helen). Berry because Headmistress of Avalon School. ------- K. B. Yabsley - 18 Worcester St, Colloroy.
SMH -- YABSLEY, 5th July, 1926, at Royal Prince Alfred Hospital, VERA WINIFRED YABSLEY, wife of Herbert W. M. Yabsley, of 32 Short St, Carlton.
FUNERAL -- The rels & friends of Herbert W. M.Yabsley & Family are invited to attend the funeral of his beloved Wife & their Mother, VERA WINIFRED YABSLEY; to leave our Chapel this Tuesday at 1.30pm, for the Congregational Cemetery, Rookwood, via Mortuary Station.
