Narrative

1851 Census Eng - Harriet Shettle was 24yr, dau of Frances M. Long, & she lived with her mother & Frederich was 2yrs, & Ruth was 8mths, & they lived in Milverton, in Warwickshire, Eng (Patience was born in 1852). Husband William wasn't on the 1851 Census.
William arrived in Sydney on the "Royal Saxon" on 13/1/1855, there is no mention of the family.
SMH - SHETTLE, 8 May 1892, at her residence, Waverley, HARRIETT, wife of William Wells Shettle, aged 65 years. Beloved mother of Frederick, Henry & Charles Shettle. No funeral notice.
