SMH - WATTS, Victor Henry (Vic) - 16 February, 2003, at a Nursing Home, late of Cordeaux Heights, formaly of Gymea Bay. Beloved husband of the late Thelma Watts. Loving father & father-in-law of Vic & Jenny, dear grandad of Mitchell & Andrew. Aged 82 years.
