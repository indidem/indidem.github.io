Narrative

SMH -- HORSEY, 5 February, 1936, at her residence, 151 Beaconsfield St, Revesby, MARIA RUTH HORSEY, loved mother of Josiah, Walter, Sarah, Arthur(dec), Lillian, Florence, Iva, Violet & Mabel, aged 85 years.
The Relatives & Friends of Mr. J.Horsey, & Miss S. Horsey, Mr.& Mrs.W.Horsey, Mrs.A.Horsey, Mr.& Mrs.R.Robinson, Mr.& Mrs,P, S.Burdon, Mr.& Mrs.H.Cherry, Mrs.V.Kingston, Mrs.M.Floyd are invited to attend the Funeral of their late beloved mother, MARIA RUTH HORSEY, late of Greenwich, North Sydney; to leave 151 Beaconsfield St, Revesby, This Monday, at 10am, for Waverley Cemetery, arriving at the cemetery gates at 11 o'clock.
