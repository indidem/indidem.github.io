13 Apr 1997, Mortdale - Gamaliel"s christening was on the 24 Apr 1804, at Saint Martin at Oak, Norwich Norfolk Eng

There were a couple of Gamaliel Thompsons but he was the only one born abt the right year! also
he was the only one born in Norfolk. IGI Batch #CO48021 Source #993659/61
Printout #0883760. I don't think I hired the film.
Gamaliel died on 10/11/1854 at Forbes St Liverpool, which I think was Liverpool Hospital, age 50yrs.
At 20yrs of age Gamaliel was charged with pickpocketing and arrived as a Convict during 1827 on the
ship "Hastings.
Gamaliel married Mary Anne O'Brien, Nurse, on 12 Aug 1841 in the Roman Catholic Church at Yass.
