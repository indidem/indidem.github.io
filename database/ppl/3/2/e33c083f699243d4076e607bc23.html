Narrative

SMH - DUBOS - Augustus Francis, 5 February 1951, at his residence - 3 Stamford Ave, Cabarita, dearly beloved husband of Irene Gwenyth Dubos & loved father of Yvonne & Graham, aged 37yrs.
SMH - DUBOS - Augustus Francis, 5 Feb, 1951, dearly loved son of Mr. Augustus Dubos, of 9 Stanley St, Putney, loved brother of Noreen (Mrs.T.Whitford), Frank, Ivy (Mrs.Urquhart), Kath (Mrs.E.Tighe) & Enid (Mrs.J.Williams), aged 37yrs. At Rest.
FUNERAL - DUBOS - The rels & friends of Mrs. Irene G.Dubos & Family are invited to attend the Funeral of her dearly beloved Husband & their dear Father AUGUSTUS FRANCIS DUBOS; to leave St. Mary's Church, Brays Rd, Concord North, this afternoon 7th Feb, after service at 1 o'clock for the Crematorium at Rookwood.
FUNERAL - DUBOS - The rels & friends of Mr.A.Dubos, Mr.& Mrs.T.Whitford, Mr.& Mrs.D.Urquhart, Mr.& Mrs.E.Tighe, Mr.& Mrs.J.Williams, & their Families are invited to attend the Funeral of their dearly loved Son, Brother, Brother-in-law & Uncle, AUGUSTUS FRANCIS DUBOS. (See Family notice).
FUNERAL - DUBOS - The rels & friends of Mr.& Mrs. G.E.Ellis & Family are kindly invited to attend the Funeral of their dearly loved Son-in-law, & Brother-in-law, AUGUSTUS FRANCIS DUBOS.
FUNERAL - DUBOS - I.O.O.F. Lodge Pride, Burwood. Officers & Members of the above Lodge are respectfully requested to attend the Funeral of their late esteemed member Bro. AUGUSTUS FRANCIS DUBOS. For details see Family notice.
"Gus" Dubos was cremated, but he is buried in the same grave as his Parents at Rookwood CofE Cemetery, Lot 231421.
