Narrative

Edwin & Amy lived in Foresters Building, Barnoldswick before they came to Australia.
Edwin Wilkinson left England - on 29 Sep 1911 aboard "Orvieto" arrived 9 Nov1911.
Lily, Mary & Frank " " " 12 Mar 1912 " "Pakena" " 26 Apr1912.
Amy & Eva " " " 2 May 1912 " "Commonwealth" " 25 Jun1912.
They lived in The Boulevarde, Lidcombe just off Joseph St, for a while before they bought the house at 50 Cumberland Road, Auburn N.S.W where they lived for the rest of their lives. The 1935 Electoral Rolls showed Edwin & Lily as Packers, & Amy & Eva as home duties. Ted died at home in 1943, aged 72 years.
