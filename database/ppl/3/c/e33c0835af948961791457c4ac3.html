Narrative

Nicholas Lock was a Carpenter, was born in Devonshire in 1849. His religion was Methodist. He died at Botany in 1932. He married in 1871 to Frances Collins when he was 22yrs old. Then later married Mary Miller in 1877 when he was 28yrs old. They had 10 children born to the 2nd marriage including Lucy.
