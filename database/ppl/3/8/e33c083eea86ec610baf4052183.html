Narrative

SMH - NEICH - 26 June, 1931, at his residence, Orrvil, Alice St, Harris Park, REGINALD EMANUEL NEICH, aged 52 years.
FUNERAL - NEICH - The rels & friends of the late REGINALD EMANUEL NEICH are kindly invited to attend his Funeral, to leave his late residence, Orrvil, Alice St, Harris Park, To-day, Saturday, at 2.30pm, for the Western Rd Cemetery, at Parramatta.
NEICH - The U.A.O. Druids Acorn Lodge No.81, Parramatta. The Officers & Members of the above Lodge are kindly invited to attend the Funeral of their late Bro., REGINALD E. NEICH; to leave his late residence this Saturday Afternoon, at 2.30 o'clock, for the Western Rd Cemetery, Parramatta.
Reginald is buried in the family grave at May's Hill Cemetery Parramatta - Section C - Row 9 lot 21. He is buried with his wife Evelyn Beatrice who died in 1918, aged 39yrs, & his parents & Grandparents.
"White marble pedestal & obelisk on sandstone plinth (5'11' x 2'2"sq) , lead letters & leaves. Reginald is on the South East Face. The Obelisk has fallen.
