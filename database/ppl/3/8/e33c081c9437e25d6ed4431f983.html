BD&M's - Thomas Sewell - 1893 - son of Thomas & Elizabeth - Newtown - #10719.
SMH - Can't find a death notice.
FUNERAL - The Friends of the late Mr. THOMAS SEWELL, are kindly invited to attend his Funeral; to move from 22 Cleveland St, Darlington, This Wednesday morning (26/7/1893), at 8.30am for Redfern Station, thence by 9.30 train for Moss Vale, thence to Sutton Forrest Cemetery.
Death Transcript #10719 says he was a Labourer, 57yrs old, he died of Chronic Alcoholism & Convultions; Mother & Fathers names are right, the Informant was James Nye, householder of 22 Cleveland St, Darlington. NOT MARRIED.!!!
There is a marriage to a Mary Hayden on BD&M's 1855/657 Vol43B, but without the Death notice in the S.M.H. I can't tell what family he had.
