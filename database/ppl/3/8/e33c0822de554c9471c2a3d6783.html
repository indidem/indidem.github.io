Narrative

Address:- 8 Julliette Avenue, Punchbowl.
During late 1980's early 1990's I spoke to Aunty Alice quite a couple of times about the family history, she seemed a lonely lady. I also went over to Rookwood Cemetry & photoed her parents grave for her, it is red marble & in very good condition, just behind the Anglican Chapel. She also told me her husbands ashes were scattered at St Barnabas Church Punchbowl where she could go & visit him there. The family were worried about her being on her own & moved her up to Norah Head Nursing Home where she died.
Wednesday 1st Oct.2003, John & Margaret Brookfield went to Aunty Alice's funeral service at Mackay Family Crematorium, Pacific Highway, Palmdale, then afterwards to their home & met all of Elva Blairs family. Aunty Ellen Geach was also there.
October 2003, John received a letter from Elva thanking them for going to the Funeral.
SMH - NEWELL, Alice May (nee Brookfield) - 28 September, 2003, at Norah Heads Nursing Home, late of Punshbowl. Devoted wife of Russ (dec), beloved sister of Elva, & sister-in-law of Jim (dec), loving Aunt of Sue, Janine & family. Loved sister-in-law of Beryl & Ed & aunt of Ray, Valerie & Bruce & their families. Aged 88years.
The rels & friends of Alice are invited to her funeral service, to-morrow Wednesday, at 1pm in the Chapel of Mackay Family Crematorium, Pacific Highway, Palmdale.
