Narrative

Gysbert 2nd worked in the Lands Dept. Allison has recorded Gysbert lived at 83 Hargrave St, Paddington in 1917, then at 15 King Edward St, Rockdale in 1920 & 1921, at 7 Rawson St, Rockdale in 1924 to 1933, he died there in 1936.
SMH - BONGERS - 19 Dec. 1936 (suddenly), Gysbert Bongers, of 7 Rawson St, Rockdale, dearly loved husband of Edith Maude, & loved father of Gysbert Sheldon, Jack Clarence & Ella Edith, aged 75 years.
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of Mrs. Edith Maude Bongers, Mr.& Mrs.G.S.Bongers, of Brisbane, Mr.& Mrs.J.C.Bongers, of Concord, & Miss Ella E.Bongers, of Rockdale, are invited to the funeral of her dearly loved husband & their dearly loved father, GYSBERT BONGERS, of 7 Rawson St, Rockdale; which will leave our Private Chapel Rockdale, this Monday at 3.15pm, for Woronora Crematorium, by road.
BONGERS - Officers & Members of the St. George Bowling & Reccretation Club are invited to the funeral of their respected fellow Member GYSBERT BONGERS; which will leave our Funeral Chapel, Rockdale, this Monday, at 3.15pm, for Woronora Crematorium, Sutherland.
