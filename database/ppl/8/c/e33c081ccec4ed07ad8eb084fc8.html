SMH -- ORR -- 3 Sept, 1937, at her residence, Northumberland St, Liverpool, SUSAN, beloved wife of William Orr, & loved mother of Eveline (dec), Stella (Mrs. Bossley), William, Harry, & Cecil, aged 89 years.
FUNERAL -- ORR -- The rels & friends of Mr. William Orr & Family, are invited to attend the Funeral of his dearly loved wife & their mother SUSAN, to leave her late residence 88 Northumberland St, Liverpool, this Saturday, at 3.30pm, for St Luke's Church, thence after the service to the Church of England, New Cemetery, Liverpool.
