Narrative

SMH - WRIGHT, 1st July, 1931, at his residence, "Tootal" Robert St, Strathfield. JOHN WRIGHT (late of 377 Sussex St, Sydney), & late Secretary of Sydney Homing Pigeon Society, relict of the late Elizabeth Wright, aged 86yrs.
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of the family of the late Mr. JOHN WRIGHT, of Tootal, Roberts St, Strathfield & late of Sussex St, Sydney, are invited to attend his funeral, which will leave St. Andrew's CofE Church, this Thursday, after a service at 2.30 o'clock, for the Church of England Cemetery (Section AAAA) Rookwood, by road. Friends travelling by Funeral Train leaving Mortuary Station, Regent St, city, at 1.52 pm, please alight at No 1 Cemetery Platform, & meet the Cortage at grave. All floral tributes to be sent direst to the church.
