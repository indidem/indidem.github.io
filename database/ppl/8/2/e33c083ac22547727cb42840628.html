Brooks, George Thomas

Birth Name Brooks, George Thomas
Gender male
Age at Death about 73 years, 8 months, 9 days

Narrative

George was the son of John William & Bridget Brooks, George was a widower & 40yrs old when he married Mary Irish. They didn't have any children.
SMH - BROOKS, Sept 10, 1918 at his residence, Petherton, East Crescent St, McMaharn's Point. GEORGE THOMAS, beloved husband of Mary Elizabeth Brooks, aged 75 years. By request no flowers.
The FUNERAL of the late Mr. GEORGE THOMAS BROOKS, will leave his late residence, East-cresent, McMahon's Point, today, at 12.15pm, for Baptist Cemetery, Rookwood. Friends, please join Funeral train, leaving Mortuary Station, Regent St, at 1.55pm.
George Brooks is buried Independant Section E, Row 17, Plot1124, right next to Charles & Martha Irish. There is no mention of his wife Mary Elizabeth.
