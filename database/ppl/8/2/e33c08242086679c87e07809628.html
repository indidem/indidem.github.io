Narrative

Great-Uncle Arthur used to work for the A.M.P. When my father, Norman Grist, first joined the A.M.P. they sent him to Tamworth. I think he boarded with the Underwoods for a while. Afterwards Uncle Arthur was transferred to the A.M.P. Office in Orange, where they spent the rest of their lives.
I think Wilma knew them up in Orange when she lived there.
SMH - UNDERWOOD, Arthur Emil Paul - 6 April, 1961, at hospital, Orange, dearly loved husband of Lily, & dear father of Frank, Clif & Irene, father-in0law Of Lily, Dulcie & Ray, loving grandfather of their children, aged 73 years, Interment at Orange this Saturday.
