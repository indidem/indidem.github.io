Narrative

SMH - COURT - 18 September, 1943, at hospital, JOSEPH COURT, of 9 Edward St, Concord, beloved husband of Blanche & loving father of Alan, Frank, Minnie, Elsie, Rita, Bert & Blanch, aged 74 years.
The FUNERAL of the late JOSEPH COURT, will leave our Chapel, 32 Railway Pde, Burwood, this Monday, at 10.45am, for the Independant Cemetery, Rookwood..
The Members of the Sydney Christadelphian Ecclesia are invited to attend the funeral of their late Member, Bro. JOSEPH COURT, will take place this day. For further particulars see Family Notice. signed B. Court.
