Narrative

SMH - TILLMAN - 9 April, 1944, at Nowra, Harold Kevin Tillman, dearly loved son of Mr.& Mrs. John Tillman, of 12a Membrey St, Granville, & loved brother of Pamela, aged 16 years.
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of Mr. & Mrs. John Tillman, & daughter Pamela, of Membrey St, Granville, are invited to the Funeral of their dearly loved son & her loved brother, HAROLD KEVIN; to leave St. Mark's Church, Granville, this Tuesday, after a service at 1.45pm, for the Church of England Cemetery, Rookwood (Section 5).
Funeral - The rels & friends of Mr. & Mrs.J.D.Tillman (Senior), Mrs.L.Parker & Family, of Burwood; Mr.& Mrs.A.Evans & Family; Mr.& Mrs.E.R.Henley & Family, of Punchbowl; Mr.& Mrs.S.R.Tillman & Family, of Long Jetty; Mr.& Mrs.S.F.Dega, of Box Hill; Mr.& Mrs.F.Tillman & Family; Mr.& Mrs.W.Liddell, & Mr.& Mrs.V.S.Tillman, are invited to attend the Funeral of their dearly loved Grandson & Nephew, Harold Kevin Tillman, see family notice.
