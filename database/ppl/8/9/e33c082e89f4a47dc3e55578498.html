Gender male
Age at Death 37 years, 9 months, 9 days

Narrative

4/4/2003 - Joy Murrin - Claude died at home, 36 Edith St, Leichhardt. He was a Fitter's Labourer on the NSW Govt. Railways.
WW2 Nominal Rolls - Private Hamilton Woodrow #NX95749, enlisted 4/5/1942 at Sutherland, Next of kin, Hazel Woodrow, Discharged 4/5/1943, Posting at Discharge - S R Wing ?? (he was only in 12mths)
His Death Transcription says " Late of Loftus Ave, Loftus" - he must have worked down at the Railway Sheds at Loftus, he was 36yrs old.
FUNERAL - WOODROW, Claude Hamilton - The rels & friends of Mrs Hazel Elizabeth Woodrow & family invite you to the Funeral of their dear Husband & Father, to leave O'Hare's Chapel, Norton St, Leichardt, this afternoon at 1.30 o'clock for Rookwood Church of England Cemetery,
