Narrative

SMH - RAINES, Bridget Doris - 26 September, 1956, of St.Aiden's, Murray Ave, Harbord, widow of the late William Raines, beloved mother of Douglas, Francis (dec), Kathleen (Mrs. Sankey), William, Carleen, Eileen (Mrs. Meimetia), & Anthony (dec).
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of the late BRIDGET DORIS RAINES, of St.Aiden's, Murray St, Harbord, are invited to her funeral, to leave the Church of Mary Immaculate, Whistler St, Manly, this Friday after prayers at 9.45am, for French's Forest Cemetery, arriving at 10.30am. Requiem Mass for the repose of her soal will be celebrated at 7am.
The rels & friends of Mrs. Carleen Meimetis & the late George Meimetis are invited to attend the Funeral of her beloved mother, DORIS RAINES, at French's Forest Cemetery, at 9.45am, after Requiem Mass at Mary Immaculate Church, Manly, at 7am.
