Birth Name Dawson, Annie Ada
Gender female
Age at Death 79 years, 11 months, 8 days

Narrative

Aunty Annie died in Strathdale Nursing Home, Strathfield. I visited her when she was there.
4/6/1988 - Rang Betty, she said they have just fixed up Aunty Annies grave at Woronora Catholic Lawn Cemetery, Aunty Annie is with Colin b1963 d1968 & Frank b1924 d1973, (Sec 2, lot 1335). Uncle Peter is with son John b1916 d1938, and grandson John (Tommies eldest) b & d1950. (Sec 3, Lot 923).
20/8/2008 - Went with Christine Johnson & took photos of Peter & Annie's graves.
SMH - Annie Ada McCann, 3rd December, 1974, at hospital, late of King St, Enfield, dear wife of the late Peter McCann, loved mother of Jack(dec), Molly, Tom, Bill, Frank(dec) & Betty, devoted mother-in-law, grandmother & great-grandmother of their families, aged 79 years. Annie's funeral service was held at St. Joseph's Catholic Church, Liverpool Rd, Enfield at 9am, then afterwards to Woronora Crematorium.
