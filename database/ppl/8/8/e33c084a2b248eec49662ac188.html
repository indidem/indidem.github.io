Narrative

SMH -- Ernest William Shuttleworth, 13 October, 1917, at his residence, The Lodge, Kelsey St, Arncliffe, beloved husband of Henrietta Shuttleworth, aged 46 years.
The friends of Mrs. Henrietta, Mr. David (O.A.S.), and Miss Jean Shuttleworth are invited to the funeral of her late beloved husband, & their father. ERNEST WILLIAM SHUTTLEWORTH; to leave his late residence, The Lodge, Kelsey St, Arncliffe, to-day at 1.45pm, for Woronora Cemetery, via Arncliffe Station.
The Members of the Arncliffe Band are kindly requested to attend the funeral of their late Treasurer, W. Shuttleworth, whick will leave the late residence at 1.45pm, for Woronora Cemetery.
