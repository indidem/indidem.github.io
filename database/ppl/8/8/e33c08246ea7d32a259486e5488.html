Narrative

Jack & Elsie were married (in 1905) for over 60 years. They didn't have any children.
Jack was a School Teacher down in the Southern Highlands. I think he taught at Mt Hunter School, I took a photo of it in October 2008. I have tried to find Jack's school records, what schools he taught in, but I haven't had any luck, I have been told that I will have to go to Kingswood Archives for them.
SMH -- IRISH, John Charles - 1st March, 1968, at hospital, Newcastle, of 15 Llewellyn St, Merewether, beloved husband of Annie Elsie, aged 90 years. Privately cremated.
