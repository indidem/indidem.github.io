Nichols, George

Birth Name Nichols, George
Gender male
Age at Death unknown

Narrative

Peters records says George was the son of John Nichols & Elizabeth Hancock, married 30 Nov 1783, Uley Gloucester. He arrived Sydney on the "Roxburgh Castle" on 26/5/1839. He died 10/10/1877 at Sydney, late of Ashfield, he is buried in Ashfield Cemetery, address - 37 Wellington St, Chippendale. SMH 11&12Oct1877.
