Narrative

Ann's father was Edward Allworth, who was a Freeholder of Boxford. Boxford is on the A1071 abt 5klm east of the A134 highway. It is a large town on the banks of Lambourn River, where a magnificent water mill still turns. There are medieval murals in the Church at Boxford.
