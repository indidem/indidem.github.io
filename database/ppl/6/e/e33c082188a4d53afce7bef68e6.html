Lambert, Joseph (Blacksmith)

Birth Name Lambert, Joseph (Blacksmith)
Gender male
Age at Death 77 years, 9 months, 30 days

Narrative

1851 Census - living with father-in-law & family, occupation Blacksmith.
1871 Census - New Road Side, Silsden, 1881 - Bolton Road, Silsden.
Joseph was 50yrs old on the 1881 Census, his 3rd wifes name was Ann & she was 39yrs old.
Margereta Longbottom age 55yrs lived with them, she was a farmers widow born Silsden. Jane now tells me his 3rd wife was Jane Ellis.
2008 - Joseph died 4th Qtr 1907 registered Skipton 9a 26 or 36 I can't read it. aged 77years. He is buried at St James Churchyard, Silsden with his 3rd wife & son Newby.
