Gray, Raymond Robert Ashcroft

Birth Name Gray, Raymond Robert Ashcroft
Gender male
Age at Death 77 years, 7 months, 22 days

Narrative

1916 E Rolls - show Raymond living with his father & sister Dolly, at Bigge St, Liverpool. Raymond was a Wool Classer. & a bachelor, He worked at H. Haigh & Sons Ltd - Collingwood Wool Scouring Works, south side of Mill Rd, between Charles St & Railway Arch, next door to Municipal Gas Works.
At the end of his life he lived with his neices, Misses Janet & Judith Ayres, in Speed St, Liverpool.
His Funeral Service was at St. Lukes Liverpool, & afterwards to Leppington Lawn Cemetery. I spoke to Janet Ayres at Carlingford about the family history, she said Uncle Ray was a darling !!
