Narrative

SMH - SHERACK - 8 July, 1928, at Auburn, JOHN PHILLIP JOSEPH SHERACK, dearly beloved husband of Gertrude Sherack, Araluen, 143 Auburn Rd, Auburn, aged 36 years. R.I.P.
FUNERAL - The Rels & Friends of Mr.& Mrs.J.Sherack & Family, of Minto, Mr.& Mrs.R.Ashcroft, of Glebe, Mr.& Mrs.C.E.New & Family, of Strathfield, Mr.& Mrs. T.R.Fitzgibbon, of Campbelltown, Mr.& Mrs. P.Fitzgibbon, of Stanmore, & Mr.& Mrs. A.L.Fitzgibbon, of Manly, are kindly invited to attend the Funeral of their late dearly-loved Son, Brother & Nephew, JOHN PHILLIP JOSEPH SHERACK; to leave his late residence, Arluen, 143 Auburn Rd, Auburn, This Afternoon at 1.30pm, for Catholic Cemetery, Campbelltown, by road, per Motor Service, Cortege will leave at Cemetery at 3pm. Friends travelling from Sydney please catch train leaving at 1.30pm.
SHERACK - The Friends of Mr. & Mrs. E. New & Mr. & Mrs. J. New are invited to attend the Funeral of their beloved Nephew & Cousin; to leave his late residence, 143 Auburn Rd, Auburn, this day, for Catholic Cemetery, Campbelltown.
