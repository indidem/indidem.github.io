Narrative

SMH - Sat,8/11/1930. - MARRIAGE - WARBRICK - IRISH Sept. 8th 1930, at St.James' CofE Croydon, by Rev. Arthur L. Wade, Rural Dean. Thomas Frederick Warbrick, Jun., eldest son of Mr. & Mrs. T.W.Warbrick of Homebush, to Veronica Helen, eldest daughter of the late Mr.J.F.Irish of Homebush, and Mrs.V.H.Irish, of Granville.
SMH -- WARBRICK, 18 February, 1942, at her residence, "Helensbrae", Hume Ave, Wentworth Falls, VERONICA HELEN WARBRICK, dearly loved wife of Thomas Frederick Warbrick, junior, & loving mother of Shirley & Gwenneth, & beloved daughter of Mrs.V.H.Irish, aged 32 years.
FUNERAL -- The rels & friends of Mr.T.F.Warbrick Junior & family are invited to attend the funeral of his dearly beloved wife & their loving mother, VERONICA HELEN WARBRICK; to leave our Chapel at Burwood, to-morrow, Friday at 10 o'clock, for Church of England Cemetery, Rookwood. Sec 12.
