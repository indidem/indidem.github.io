Dugdale, Ellen “Nellie”

Birth Name Dugdale, Ellen “Nellie”
Gender female

Narrative

Nellie has put an autograph in Lills Autograph Book on page 44.
" To contradict I hate such ways, Tis rude tis very wrong,
No matter what a woman says, a man should hold his tongue."
signed Nellie Dugdale, 34 Skipton Rd, Barnoldswick.
Nellie was a Cotton Weaver, & lodged with Joseph & Betty Green in 1881 Census.
