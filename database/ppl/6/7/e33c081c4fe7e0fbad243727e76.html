Procter, Thomas

Birth Name Procter, Thomas
Gender male
Age at Death 95 years, 2 months, 6 days

Narrative

Thomas arrived 31 March, 1861.on "Queen Bee" aged 13yrs, with mother & siblings, he could read & write. He worked as Wheelwright & Coachbuilder for Blane P/L Charlton St, Wickham. After they went bankrupt middle 1904, he worked as a traveller (1908-12) then a watchman. His 90th birthday was in the Newcastle Herald.
