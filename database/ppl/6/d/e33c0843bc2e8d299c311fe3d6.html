Narrative

Isobel was born abt 1901 in Comara NSW, she was the daughter of Christopher McIntyre & Margaret Mary Higgins. She worked as a nurse & lived in Darlinghurst when she was married in 1923.
SMH - SIMMONDS (Pund), Isobel, 16 August, 1981, at Toonbabbie, loved mother of Mona, Patricia, John & Gordon (dec), dear mother-in-law, grandmother & g-grandmother of their families. In her 82nd year. Privately cremated at Castlebrook Memorial Gardens & Crematorium, Rouse Hill.
