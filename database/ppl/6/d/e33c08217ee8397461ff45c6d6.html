Narrative

1881 Census - Jeremiah & Mary lived at 60 Chapeltown Rd, Leeds. They had 5 children & Booth Wilkinson, nephew b1868, born Silsden, aged 13yrs, & Martha Bradley b1858, servant 23yrs, born Northallerton, lived with them. Jeremiah was a Master Grocer.
Jane has given me a photo, taken in the Beckett Street Cemetery, Leeds -- The beloved Jeremiah Booth Dawson, who died December 17th, 1898, aged 56 years. (his wife name is not on the headstone)
