Narrative

SMH - 12th November, 1932.- George Frederick NOAD(late headmaster of Liverpool Junior Technical School), beloved husband of Brenda Marguerite Noad, & father of Llyris, of 14 Merlin St, Roseville, aged 47 years.
NOAD - The relatives & friends of Mrs. Brenda M. NOAD & daughter Llyris, are invited to the FUNERAL of her beloved Husband & her Father, George Frederick NOAD to leave our Private Chapel, this morning (14/11/1932) after a service at 10.15 o'clock, for the Northern Suburbs Methodist Cemetery.
NOAD - The relatives & friends of Mrs. George F.NOAD, Senior, & Mr.& Mrs.J.J.Noad (of Hurlstone Park), Mr.& Mrs.H.Noad (of Dulwich Hill), Mr.& Mrs.S.T.Noad (of Concord), Messrs. Carl, Raymond & Allan Myers, & the Misses Olive, Doris & Dulcie Myers (of Richmond), Mrs.Mary Myers (of Meadowbank) & Mr.& Mrs.H.Moxley (of Brisbane) are invited to attend the Funeral of her Son, their Brother & Brother-in-law, George Frederick NOAD, to leave our Chapel at Crows Nest, this morning (14/11/1932) after a Service at 10.15am, for Northern Suburbs Methodist Cemetery.
