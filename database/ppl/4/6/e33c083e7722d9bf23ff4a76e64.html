SMH - ASHCROFT - Catherine Clara, 15 April, 1957, late of Forbes, at her daughters residence, 12 Noora St, Lidcombe, widow of the late Frederick Charles Ashcroft, loved mother of Roy (Panania), Kath (Mrs.Toogood, Lidcombe), William (Manildra), Harry (Wyong), Frank (Strathfield), Jim (Cabramatta), Becky (Mrs.Nicholson, Lidcombe), aged 85 years.
