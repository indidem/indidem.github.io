Narrative

Stanley Ellis was born 2 Nov, 1892 at Carlisle St, Enfield.
WW1 Nominal Rolls - Stan was Private #551, 2nd MG Battalion A.I.F.- Enlisted 11/6/1916, RTA 7/7/1919.
SMH - ELLIS - 14 November, 1936, at Penrith, Stanley Ellis, dearly loved brother of Jennie & George, late A.I.F., aged 44 years.
ELLIS - The rels & friends of Mr.& Mrs.A.Wilson & Mr.& Mrs.G.Ellis, are invited to attend the Funeral of their dearly loved Brother, STANLEY ELLIS, late of Oakura St, Rockdale, will leave the Returned Soldiers'Memorial Hall, Bay St, Rockdale, this Monday, after a service, which will commence at 2.30pm, for Woronora Crematorium. By request, no flowers.
ELLIS - The Officers & Members of Rockdale R.S.S.I.L.A., are invited to attend the Funeral of their late Member, STANLEY ELLIS, see family notice.
