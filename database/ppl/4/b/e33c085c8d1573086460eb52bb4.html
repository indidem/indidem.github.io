Narrative

SMH - STREET, 23 July, 1925, at her home, "Allowah" Waratah St, Hurstville, Mary Jane, beloved wife of Snelling Francis Street, loving mother of Cleve, Col, & Vic, in her 75th year. The rels & friends of Mr. Snelling Street, of Waratah St, Hurstrivve, & Messrs Cleve, Colin & Vistor Street, are invited to attend the Funeral of his late wife & their mother, Mary Jane Street, this afternoon, on arrival of the 2.15pm Funeral train from Central Station, to the Church of England Cemetery, Woronora.
The rels & friends of Mrs. Alfred Stafford, Mrs T. Lawson, Mrs.D.Robertson, & Mrs.H.Harper, are invited to attend the Funeral of their dear Sister, Mary Jane Street, to take the funeral train to the CofE Section of Woronora Cemetery.
