Irish, Albert Septimas

Birth Name Irish, Albert Septimas
Gender male
Age at Death 34 years, 9 days

Narrative

Albert Irish lived next door to Aunty Hilda in Ashfield. Aunty Hilda had their birthdates in her diary. Rang Australian Society of Authors & left a message for Lola to ring me. Lola said she remembers Aunty Hilda. Lola said her mother had flaming red hair, she said she looked "Irish", her grandchildren have the same red hair. Lola said her father was treated for T.B. at Waterfall Sanitorium.
