SMH - GRAY, PHYLLIS OLIVE - 9 Feb, 1982 (suddenly0, at R.G.H. Concord, late of Revesby, dearly loved wife of the late Sydney Gray, loved mother of James, Anne (dec) & Anthony, fond mother-in-law of Maureen, & dear grandmother of Julie, Catherine, Malcolm, Stephen, Joanne & Sharon, loved sistewr of Patricia & Winifred. Mass commencing at 9am. The relatives & friends are invited to attend the funeral, to leave the Church after prayers following the Mass for the Catholic Lawn Cemetery, at Rookwood.
