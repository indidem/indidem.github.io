Narrative

Found this family on 1881 Census living in Wolverhampton. Film 1341670 RG 2799 Folio 74 Page 6.
Samuel was 40yrs old, Eliza was 39, & their children were 12, 9, 5, & 2yrs old.
Samuel was an Inspector of ?.
I found a marriage for a Frederick W. Blanton at Wolverhampton in 1st Qtr 1900, when I was looking
for Richard Blanton's marriage. It would make him 31yrs old. There doesn't look like there's another
Frederick W. Blanton on the 1881 Census in Wolverhampton!!
I found Samuel's death when I was looking for Henry Edwards death. Samuel's death corresponds
with his age 57yrs.
