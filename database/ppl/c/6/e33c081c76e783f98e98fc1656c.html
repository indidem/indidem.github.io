SMH - TIPPING, ABRAHAM THOMAS, 3 May, 1949, at Randwick, & late of Bathurst & Bexley North, dearly beloved husband of Lillian Lucy & loving father & father-in-law of Norman & Nina, Lucy & Ken, & Tom, & dear grandfather of Patsy Anne, Norma, Ian, Tom & Graham, & dear brother of John (Penrith), Herb (Manly), & Septimus (Queensland), aged 74 years.
FUNERAL - TIPPING - The Rels & Friends of Mrs.Lillian Tipping & Family, of Bexley North, are invited to attend the Funeral of her dearly beloved Husband & their loving Father, Father-in-law, Grandfather & Brother, ABRAHAM THOMAS TIPPING (late of Bathurst) to leave our Chapel, this Wednesday, after a service at 10.45am, for the Eastern Suburbs Crematorium.
