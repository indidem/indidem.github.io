Birth Name Ackroyd, Harriett
Gender female
Age at Death 55 years, 7 months, 19 days

Narrative

Harriett & Annie & Frank came to Aust. together on the S.S. Belgravia arrived 3/5/1884.
24/3/1987 - applied for Harriett's Death Cert. She died Western Suburbs Cottage Hospital, formally of Dean St, Enfield, 55yrs old. Husband George Ellis was the informant. There were 5 children living & 1 deceased.
I have advertised in the Telegraph RSVP for the Ellis Family.
SMH - ELLIS - 25 June, 1915, at Western Suburbs Hospital, Croydon, HARRIET, beloved wife of G. E. Ellis, of Enfield, aged 55 years.
FUNERAL - ELLIS - The rels & friends of Mr.G.E.Ellis & George, Stanley & Walter Ellis are invited to attend the Funeral of his dear wife & their mother, HARRIETT ELLIS; to leave Esholt, Dean St, Enfield, on Monday (28/6/1915) at 2.30 o'clock, for St Thomas's C of E Cemetery, Enfield.
