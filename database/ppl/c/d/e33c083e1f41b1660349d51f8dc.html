SMH - BOYER - 17 June, 1934, at her daughters residence, 61 Cross St, Guildford, ELIZABETH CHARLOTTE, relict of the late John B. Boyer, & loving mother of Mrs.J.Sherack (Minto), Mr.J.Boyer (Minto), & Mrs. Bert Piggott (Guildford), aged 85yrs.FUNERAL - The Friends of MR.& Mrs.J.Sherack & Family of Minto, Mr.& Mrs.Bert Piggott & Family of Guildford, Mr.& Mrs.John Boyer & Family of Minto, are invited to attend the Funeral of their Mother & Grandmother, ELIZABETH CHARLOTTE BOYER, to leave her late residence 61 Cross St, Guildford, at 1.30 this Monday afternoon, for the Congregational Cemetery at Campbelltown.
