Narrative

Feb 2017 - Allison & George had a weeks holiday in Norfolk Island, where she tried to do some research on Fletcher Quintrell, but couldn't find him there.
1891 Census - Fletcher was 3yrs old & lived with his parents Sam & Mary Ann Quintrell, St. Columb, Cornwell,P1819-F24.
Departure from England to Australia - 17 October, 1912
1913 Census - Labourer at Bradjerribong, in Parkes.
What did Fletcher do during 1st WWar? that he met & married Maggie un Auburn.
11 June, 1921 - Married at Auburn Baptist Church #9484.
1930 Census - was living with Maggie & was an Ice Vendor (or Labourer) in Lakemba.
1932, 1933, 1936, 1937, 1943 Census's - all living in Lakemba.
1949 Census - Fletcher was an Ice Vendor in Speers Point, Maggie was not with him.
1954 Census - Fletcher was Ice Vendor in Croudace Bay, but no Maggie.
1958, 1963, 1968 Census - Fletcher was Ice Vendor lived in Valentine, Maggie had joined him.
1972 Census - called Vendor?, Electoral Roll changed to remove Maggie, Maggie died August 1972.
1977 Census - Fletcher lived in Masonic Village Nursing Home at Edgeworth.
