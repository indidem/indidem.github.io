Narrative

My Family TREE says Mary Tyer daughter of Edward Tyer was born 16 May 1786 and died 10 Feb 1857 She Lived for another 15yrs after her husband died. I haven't had time to look at St Catherines to see
where she died. I haven't looked at the I.G.I. to see if her birth was recorded.
The I.G.I. says Henry Cook married Mary Marven Syer on 25 Oct 1809 at Holton St. Marys, & Mary Jane
Marven Cook was Christened on 25 Feb1810 at Stratford Saint Mary the daughter of Henry Cook & Mary Marvin Syer.
10/3/2005 - Received from Shirley Wharton, Mary Marven Cook's Will, dated 20/3/1851.
Robert Keningale Cook son, and George Keningale Cook nephew, were Execators of the will.
It looks like she lived at Church Street ...... ..... Colchester, Essex.
16/3/2005 - looked for 4hrs at Mortdale for her in the 1851 census, in Essex but couldn't find her.
Found the 1881 census for Robert Kenningale Cook & family, in Smallbridge. Also found Mary Jane Cook & William Frost in Stoke by Nayland and Photocopied them both.
30/3/2005 - Looked last week & this week for the marriages for Henry & Mary Cook's children, 1838 to 1845, so far only found Robert's marriage in 1844, & Hannah's marr to Magnus Wolfson in 1842.
6/4/05 - Found Marriage for:- George Keningale Cook in 4th Qtr 1847 - Samford X11 853.
" " " Mary Keningale Cook " " " " -- Witham X11 597.
