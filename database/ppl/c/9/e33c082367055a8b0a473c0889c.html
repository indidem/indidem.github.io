Narrative

I remember Aunty Ruby. She used to work at Winns in Oxford St. She was a widow for a long time & she probably worked for a living. We met her at Aunty Mabels place when we were on our honeymoon in 1952. Keith remembers us playing cards with Aunty Ruby at Aunty Mabels place.
