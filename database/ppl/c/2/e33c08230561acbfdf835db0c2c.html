Small, William Henry Arthur

Birth Name Small, William Henry Arthur
Gender male
Age at Death 80 years, 4 months, 16 days

Narrative

"Bill" Small was born in Wennington near Rainham, Essex, Eng. His family came to Aust. when he was about 2or3yrs old. He died at his daughters place at Berwick Park, Wilton near Picton, NSW Aust.
Hilda & Bill lived at Burnham Grove, Strathfield, then Howard St Strathfield for many years.
SMH - SMALL - William Henry Arthur - 28 October, 1990, at hospital, formally of Howard St, Strathfield, widower of Hilda Olive, loved father of Wilma & Alwyn Bullock, of Wilton, NSW, & fond grandfather of Robert & Edward Wray.
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of Mr. WILLIAM HENRY ARTHUR SMALL are kindly invited to attend his funeral, to leave the Church of Christ Church, Clarence St, Burwood, to-morrow after a service at 2.30pm, for Rookwood Crematorium.
SMALL - The Officers & Brethren of Lodge Homebush No.910, U.G.L. of N.S.W., are fraternally invited to attend the funeral of their late esteemed Brother, Very Wor. Bro. WILLIAM HENRY ARTHUR SMALL, P.D.G.I.W., to be held at 2.30pm, on Thursday at Church of Christ, Clarence St, Burwood.
Raymond Small died at home, 12 mths old. There was no death notice in SMH.
