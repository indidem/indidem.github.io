Narrative

1926 Electoral Rolls - Eliza Ashcroft - "Dellwood", Moore St, Liverpool, H.D.
SMH - ASHCROFT - 18 September, 1931, at her residence, Moore St, Liverpool, ELIZA, widow of the late Edward James Ashcroft, aged 79 yrs. At Rest.
SMH - ASHCROFT - 18 Sept, 1931, at her residence, Moore St, Liverpool, ELIZA ASHCROFT, dearly loved mother of Amy (Mrs.Andrews), Ted, Eva (Mrs.Prince), Leslie, Tom, Elsie (Mrs.Leake), Cecil, & Beattie (Mrs.Fitzpatrick).
FUNERAL - The Rels & Friends of the late Mrs. ELIZA ASHCROFT are invited to attend her Funeral; to leave St. Luke's Church, Liverpool, This Saturday, after a short service at 3pm, for the Church of England (new) Cemetery, Liverpool. Eliza was the grand-daughter of William Fowler, who had the original holding where Wynyard station now stands.
