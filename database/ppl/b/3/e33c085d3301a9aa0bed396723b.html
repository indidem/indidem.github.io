Narrative

Concord Public School - The annual Distribution of prizes took place on Friday, at Concord Public School. Alderman Hillier (ex Mayor of Concord) presided. The Master (Mr. Thomas Cambourn) was presented by the upper class with an address & a silk-bound edition of Tennyson. Mrs. Cambourn was presented with a salad bowl.
