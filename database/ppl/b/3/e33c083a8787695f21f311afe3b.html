Narrative

SMH - BUTLER _ November 25, 1904, at Prince Alfred Hospital, CORNELIUS BUTLER, beloved husband of Annie Louisa Butler, & youngest son of Mrs.C.F.Butler, of Summer Hill, in his 42nd year.
FUNERAL - BUTLER - The Friends of the deceased Mr. CORNELIUS BUTLER are respectfully invited to attend his Funeral; to leave his late residence, 34 Morris St, Summer Hill, this afternoon, at 2 o'clock, for the Necropolis, via Summer Hill Station.
FUNERAL - BUTLER - The Members of Hope of Petersham Division Sons & Daughters of Temperance are respectfully invited to attend the Funeral of our late Brother, Cornelus BUTLER; to move from his late residence, 34 Morris St, Summer Hill, this day, Saturday, at 2pm, from Summer Hill Station, thence to the Necropolis.
