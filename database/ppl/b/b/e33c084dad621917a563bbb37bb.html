Narrative

Elizabeth was born in 1865 in Beechworth, Vic., she died in 1914 in Hall St., Spotswood, Vic. from thrombosis in her legs, & a pumonary embolism (6 weeks). She is buried in in St Kilda Wesleyan Cemetery, Comp A, Grave 52. with her baby son Jack who died in 1901.
