Birth Name Owen, Marjorie “Dawn”

Age at Death 66 years, 11 months, 28 days

Narrative

"Dawn" was the daughter of Charles Benjamin Owen & Leila Bird, She was born 1923 at 4 Brooks Cres, Hawthorn, Vic, she was educated at Tintern CofE Grammar School in Glenferrie Rd, Hawthorn, she died 1990 in Canberra Hospital of Metastatic Adenocarcinoma of breast (2.5yrs), Lymphangitis carcinomatosis (2mths) & respiratory failure (2weeks), she was 67yrs old. Her ashes were spread over Tangalwangalow Forest, Canberra.
