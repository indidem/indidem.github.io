Narrative

SMH -- Sidney Parkes, died 19 July, 1958, at hospital, late of 424 Guildford Rd, Guildford, & Junee, dear husband of Jessie, loved father of Cath, Henry, William & David, loved grandfather of their children, aged 71 years.
The relatives of Sidney Parkes are invited to his funeral, at Labour Funerals, Auburn,this morning at 10 o'clock, & then the Presbyterian Cemetery at Woronora.
The rels of John, Jessie, Grace & Alick & Lily Parkes are invited to the funeral of their brother, Sidney Parkes, to leave Labour Funerals, Auburn, after a service to-day, for Woronora Cemetery.
The Officers & Brethren of Lodge Holroyd, No.750, U.G.L. of N.S.W. are requested to attend the funeral of their late Brother....
The Officers & Members of Guildford Lodge P.A.F.S. of A. are invited to attend the funeral of their late Brother.....
