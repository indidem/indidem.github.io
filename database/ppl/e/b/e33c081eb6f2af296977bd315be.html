Narrative

SMH -- Jean Graham SHUTTLEWORTH, 20 July, 1923, at her daughters residence, Harrington, Albion St, Marrickville, loved mother of M. E. W. S. & Bert Shuttleworth & Mrs. H.A.Stores, Mrs.A.J.Sadlier, & Mrs.H.Mason, aged 80 years.
The rels & friends of Mrs. JEAN GRAHAM SHUTTLEWORTH are invited to her Funeral; to leave her daughters residence, Albion St, Marrickville, this afternoon, at 1.30pm, for Methodist Cemetery, Woronora, via Sydenham Station.
