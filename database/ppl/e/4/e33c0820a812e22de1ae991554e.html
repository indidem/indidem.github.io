Connor, Stephen James

Age at Death 83 years, 6 months, 5 days

Narrative

Stephen James Connor.- born 16 Dec 1894 at Sydney St, Willoughby NSW. Served as a Naval Chief Petty Officer during World War 11, & later as an Engineer with the Sydney County Council.
They lived at Kings Cross during the Depression, then at Strathfield, Canberra, Lapstone & Hornsby.
SMH - Stephen James Connor - 21 June, 1979, at hospital, late of Muriel St, Hornsby, loved husband of Mabel Esther, loved father & father-in-law of Julie & Don, Ron & Lyn & John & Joy, aged 85 years. Stephen's Funeral Service was in the North Chapel of Northern Suburbs Crematorium, next Monday at 1pm. Lot 45RM.
