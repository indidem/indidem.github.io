Narrative

I don't know when Albert married Annie McKendrick, Qld records don't go that far, but I wrote to Janet Reakes asking if she could find the death of Alfred somewhere in the early 1960's but all she could find was Annie's death on 16/7/1968, aged 74 years.
I have a letter dated Xmas 1964 from Aunty Annie giving me a pair of booties when Susie was born! & telling me about all the things she is doing for Christmas.
