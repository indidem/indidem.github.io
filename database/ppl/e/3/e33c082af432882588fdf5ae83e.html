SMH - THORNTON - 30 November, 1927, at her residence, King St, Randwick, Sarah Thornton, relict of the late Sydney Clyde Thornton, of Randwick, & dearly loved mother of Emily, Rose, Daisy, Sydney, George & Arthur, on the eve of her 85th birthday.
FUNERAL - The rels & friends of Mr.& Mrs.Sydney Thornton of Coogee, Mr.& Mrs.F.B.Dixon of Randwick, Mr.& Mrs.George Thornton of Randwick, Mrs. Annie Thornton of Coogee, Mr.& Mrs.Thomas Combe of Rockdale, Mr.& Mrs.Arthur Thornton of Marrickville, and Mr.& Mrs.J.G.Yuill of Randwick, are invited to the Funeral of their dearly loved Mother, SARAH THORNTON; to leave her residence, 129 King St, Randwick, this Friday, at 10.30am, for Randwick General Cemetery (per Motor Service).
