Narrative

I have the photocopy of the Parish Records of St Peters, Campbelltown, County of Cumberland, #1141 Vol27a, that shows HARRY WINTON was Born on the 21st May, 1843, & Baptised on the 23rd of Something, 1843, his parents were William & Mary Anna Winton, of Campbelltown, Farmer, & the Minister was George Nidal, there is no record of the month the baptism took place so I recorded it as June.
SMH - WINTON - 21 March 1921, at his residence "Windmill", Appin, HARRY WINTON, dearly beloved husband of Susannah, & father of Edward, Hilder, Alice, Leslie, Nellie & Charlie, aged 77 years.
FUNERAL - WINTON - The Rels & friends of Mrs. Susannah Winton & Family, are kindly invited to attend the Funeral of her dearly loved husband & their father, HARRY, to leave Windmill Hill, at 3.30pm This Tuesday Afternoon, for the Church of England Cemetery, Appin.
Harry Winton was Church Warden of St. Marks Anglican Church, Appin from 1890 to 1901. Most of the children were baptised & buried there.
