Narrative

26/11/1988 - found Uncle Ernest's birthdate on St.Catherines - Reg. 4th Qtr 1879 Leicester 7a 244.
1881 Census - They lived at 3 Humberstone Gate 1 Court A, St Margarets, Leicester.
26 Oct 1988 - I applied for the Marriage Cert. of Ernest & Mabel Golby on 23/6/1908, Church of Christ Chapel, Paddington. Ruby Pascoe & Leonard Lewis were the witnesses (before they were married).
I used to love to visit Aunty Mabel & Uncle Ern. They lived in the house in the street at the back of Grandma's. I have done all the Golby & Thornton family history, Sylvester Thornton was a Convict.
