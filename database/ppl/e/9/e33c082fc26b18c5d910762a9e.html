Watson, Robert Scarlet (Painter)

Birth Name Watson, Robert Scarlet (Painter)
Gender male
Age at Death 45 years, 3 months, 4 days

Narrative

July 2005 - I have the Baptism Records for Robert Scarlet Watson, his birth was 12 Feb,1863 and the Baptism was 1st March 1863, I think it was at Scots Presbyterian Church, Sydney by Rev John Dunmore Lang, they lived in Cumberland St, & his father Samuel Watson was a Grocer.

5/6/2005 - Death Transcription from Joy Murrin.
WATSON, Robert Scarlet - 16 May, 1908, at Victoria, Park Ave, Drummoyne, Painter, Informant - A.E.Watson, son, of Park Ave, Drummoyne. He died of Pulmonary Tuberculosis for 3 yrs. Parents- Samuel Watson Grocer, & Mary Ann Robertson, Buried on 17 May, 1908 at Field of Mars, Presbyterian Cemetery. Witness- Henry Watts & Robert H. Cook. Aged 45yrs 3mths. Married at Rozelle Sydney to Lillian Victoria Cook. Children of Marriage:- Arthur Edward 22, Elizabeth Ivy 20, Eileen Sarah 16, Ronald Sydney 14, Winifred Cook 12, Robert Neil 9, Living, 2 Females deceased.
