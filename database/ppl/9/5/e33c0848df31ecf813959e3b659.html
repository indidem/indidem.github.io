Narrative

SMH - William Pierce MANNELL - 31st May, 1935, at his residence, Westcarville" Phillip St, Lakemba; ex-Sergeant of Police, beloved husband of Caroline Ann Mannell, aged 74yrs.
SMH - William Pierce MANNELL - The relatives & friends of Mrs. Caroline MANNELL & Family are invited to the Funeral of their dearly loved Husband & Father; to leave his late residence, "Westcarville, Phillip St, Lakemba, this Saturday Afternoon (1/6/1935) at 3 o'clock, for Moorefields Cemetery.
SMH - MANNELL - P.A.F.S. of A. Lakemba Lodge No.147, The Officers & Members are respectfully requested to attend the Funeral of their late esteemed Bro., William Pierce MANNELL, See Family Notice.
