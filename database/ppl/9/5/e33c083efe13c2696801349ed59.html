Braithwaite, Edward

Birth Name Braithwaite, Edward
Gender male
Age at Death 69 years, 10 months, 8 days

Narrative

Edward arrived on the Minerva 5 on 19/11/1824.
The 1st four children were born at Orphan School Rd, now Mulgoa Rd, 4 miles from Liverpool. The last five children were born at "Ruthvale" near Kemps Creek, near Bringelly. He had 6sons & 3daughters !!
Henry lived "Graceville" near South Creek, Daniel lived in Liverpool, Mrs.E.J.J.Hamon lived in Macquarie St, Mrs G.Hamon lived St. Peters, Thomas in Belligan, Edward at Exeter Farm.
Edward Braithwaite was a shoemaker. He was 69yrs old when he died.He is buried at Rossmore.
