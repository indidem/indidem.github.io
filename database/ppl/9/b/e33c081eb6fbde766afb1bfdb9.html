Birth Name Shuttleworth, Mark Edmond (Police)

Age at Death 83 years, 11 months, 15 days

Narrative

Mark E. Shuttleworth migrated to New Zealand abt 1858/1860. Then he moved to Australia from Auckland on the "Lord Worsley" on 8 May, 1860. From the Police archives Mark was listed as Edward M. Shuttleworth, reg.# 1141, he was a mounted policeman, aged 25yrs, 5' 9", blue eyes, brown hair, fresh complection, & native of England. He was single, his previous occupation was storekeeper, religion Church of England.

Mark E. Shuttleworth Senior died aged 84yrs at Rookwood (Lidcombe) Asylum.
