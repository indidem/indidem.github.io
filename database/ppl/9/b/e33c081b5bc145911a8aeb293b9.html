Narrative

Ruth Lord - b 6 May 1812 Manchester, Lancashire Eng (perhaps 1811-1813) alt to 24 Apr 1812 !!!
Baptised 20 May 1820 St Johns CofE Parramatta - (1813/#5121-1B) NSW BD&M's (8yrs old)
Arrived with Convict mother Ann Lord per Emu 1812, Isabella 1813 & Broxbornbury 28 July, 1814.
Married - 7 Jan 1828, St Johns CofE Parramatta, #4206-3 & #605-12; marriage aged 15-17, varies with documents.
DIED - 3 Jun 1866, Parramatta, aged 54yrs, #5894.
Buried - St Johns CofE Cemetery, Parramatta, with husband.
1828 Census - listed as wife of Joseph Gray, lodger, at Jas.Kenny, Parramatta. Ruth had 10 Children.
