Tillman, Samuel

Birth Name Tillman, Samuel
Gender male
Age at Death 69 years, 11 months, 21 days

Narrative

Samuel Tillman Shipwright & Margaret Jane (Davison) Tillman & six children arrived on the
"Northbrook" arrived Sydney on 10 March 1878. His father also Samuel Tillman was a Potter.
When Margaret died in 1906 they lived at 44 Hanover Street, Waterloo, Sydney, they had another six children in Sydney.
After Margaret died, Samuel married Elizabeth Clelland on the 9 Nov 1909, she was a widow, her father was James Pearson, her mother was Elizabeth Woodward.
Samuel & Margaret are buried at Rookwood CofE cemetry Sec 5 lot 1004, Fiche #178918 there is also a
female baby buried with them. I saw the grave & took a photo about 1994, but I can't find the photo.
I haven't had time to check Probate files yet, also haven't checked SMH & Rookwood Cemetry yet.
Nov 2002 - Nancy Ringland rang, 925 Toms Creek Rd, Long Flat 2426, she reconds all Tillmans in Aust. are related.
