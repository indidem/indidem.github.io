Narrative

Ozzie was the son of George Montgomery & Agnes Bourke.
Ozzie lived in Willoughby in 1923 when he was married. He was a bricklayer.
SMH - MONTGOMERY, Oswald - 4th January, 1976, at hospital, formally of Willoughby, dearly loved husband of the late Dorothy, loved father of June, dear father-in-law of Lance, loving Pop of Jim & Joanne. Rest in peace, Privately interred.
Ozzie died only two & a half months after Dot, & as they were both privately "Interred" I don't know which cemetery they are in. I have checked the micro-fiche's for "Field of Mars" & "North Rocks" but their not there. I'll have to ring Northern Suburbs Cemetery now called Macquarie Park to see if they are there.
