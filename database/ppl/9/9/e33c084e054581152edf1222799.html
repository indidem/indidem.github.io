Brick, Edward

Birth Name Brick, Edward
Gender male
Age at Death 63 years, 4 months, 24 days

Narrative

Edward was the son of John Brick & Ellen Wilson. He worked as a Newsagent in 1890, & he lived at 189 Cecil St, Sth Melbourne, He was working as a Manager in 1917 in Ballarat, & lived in Ballarat, Vic. when he was married. He worked as a photographer (Sporting Globe?) in 1928 in Melbourne. He died in 1929 in "Palice Court", Beaconsfield Parade, St Kilda, from cardiac degeneration & Syncope (sudden) Post Mortem was held. He was buried in Coburg Roman Catholic Cemetery, Compartment P,grave 768.
Louisa & Edward had no children.
