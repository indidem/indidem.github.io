Applied for Marriage of Frederick & Emily 18 July 2003.
7th February 1878, Register Office, district of Skipton, County of York. Frederick was 22yrs, Bach., Bookkeeper from Addington, father Abraham Emmott, (dec), Grocer, & Emily Hartley, 24yrs, Spinster, Power Loom Weaver. from Addington, father George Hartley, Coal Dealer. One of the Signatures was Ellis Hartley ?? a brother born abt 1856 in Addington.
