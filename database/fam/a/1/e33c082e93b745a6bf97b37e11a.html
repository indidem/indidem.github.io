Narrative

10/4/03, applied to Joy Murrin for transcript #02221, George Kitchener Hatton, age 19, of Leichhardt, Sydney, occupation dealer, son of Arthur Herbert Hatton & Margaret Hannah Buckley, father - dealer, was Married on 19th February, 1936, at All Soul's Church of England, Leichhardt - to:- Doris Lillian Woodrow, Spinster of Sydney, Home Duties, age 21 years of Leichhardt, Parents John Brown Woodrow & Christina Bongers, Father - Printer. Witnesses A. Hatton & J. Woodrow (fathers). Minister Rev. Leland Parsons. Arthur H. & Margaret H. Hatton, parents of the Groom gave consent to the Marriage, (because the groom was under 21 years)
