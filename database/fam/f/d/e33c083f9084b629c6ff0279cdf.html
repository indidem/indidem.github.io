Narrative

Edward & Margaret were married at St. Thomas's Mulgoa, on 4 Apr 1864. It is a beautiful old sandstone building with a flagstone floor, situated just off the Mulgoa Rd, on the Penrith side of Mulgoa. The marriage was conducted by Rev. George Vidal, who was second minister appointed to the parish.
